TOUR OVERVIEW
Empower students with an immersive journey into the heart of Mizoram's culture, geography, and history. This
TRAGUIN curated educational expedition offers school groups a structured path to explore Northeast India’s
rich diversity, engaging with local traditions, heritage sites, and academic environments, creating
unforgettable memories.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL & ORIENTATION
Arrive in Aizawl. Private group transfer to your educational basecamp hotel. Evening briefing session on
Mizoram’s geography and itinerary highlights.

DAY 02 | MIZO CULTURAL HERITAGE
A structured day visiting the Mizoram State Museum and traditional handicraft centers, focusing on Mizo
history, social customs, and indigenous art.
DAY 03 | ACADEMIC & INSTITUTIONAL INSIGHTS
A guided visit to local educational institutions, engaging with academic environments and learning about
Mizoram’s educational advancements.
DAY 04 | NATURE & ENVIRONMENTAL STUDIES
Explore the local environment with a guided field trip, observing unique mountain flora and learning about
sustainable ecological practices in the hills.
DAY 05 | DEPARTURE
Final group breakfast, review of educational findings, and private transfer to the airport, completing your
premium school tour.
