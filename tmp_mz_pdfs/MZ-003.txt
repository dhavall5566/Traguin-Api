TOUR OVERVIEW
Discover the charm, culture, and scenic beauty of Aizawl, the capital of Mizoram, with this TRAGUIN curated
family getaway. Designed for a blend of cultural exploration and relaxation, this Aizawl Special itinerary offers
a serene escape into Northeast India’s mountain lifestyle. Experience warm hospitality with TRAGUIN's 
handpicked stays, creating unforgettable memories.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL
Arrive in Aizawl. Private transfer to your handpicked hotel. Enjoy the evening exploring local markets and
mountain views.
DAY 02 | CULTURE & MUSEUMS
Visit the Mizoram State Museum and local handicraft centres at Luangmual to learn about traditional Mizo life
and iconic attractions.
