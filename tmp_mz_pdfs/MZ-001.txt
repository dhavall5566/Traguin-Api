TOUR OVERVIEW
Discover the hidden hills, lush landscapes, and vibrant culture of Mizoram with this TRAGUIN curated family
discovery. Designed for exploration and comfort, this Mizoram Discovery itinerary offers a serene escape
into the heart of Northeast India’s natural beauty. Experience the warm hospitality of Mizoram with
TRAGUIN's handpicked stays, creating unforgettable memories.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL
Arrive in Aizawl, the capital of Mizoram. Private transfer to your handpicked hotel. Enjoy the evening soaking
in the breathtaking mountain views.
DAY 02 | AIZAWL CITY TOUR
Explore the cultural highlights of Aizawl. Visit local markets, heritage centres, and museums. Experience the
local Mizo culture and iconic attractions.

DAY 03 | REIEK MOUNTAIN & HERITAGE VILLAGE
Embark on an excursion to Reiek Mountain. Explore the heritage village, showcasing traditional Mizo homes
and culture—a premium experience for the whole family.
DAY 04 | VANTAWNG FALLS & NATURAL WONDERS
Visit the spectacular Vantawng Falls, the highest waterfall in Mizoram. This immersive experience is a must-
see for all nature lovers.
DAY 05 | TAMDIL LAKE LEISURE
Spend a day enjoying the scenic beauty of Tamdil Lake. Perfect for a peaceful day out, collecting
unforgettable memories in the hills.
DAY 06 | DEPARTURE
Final breakfast before your TRAGUIN private transfer to the airport, completing your premium Mizoram
experience.
