TOUR OVERVIEW
Unleash your spirit of adventure with this high-energy TRAGUIN curated expedition. Designed for thrill-
seekers, this Mizoram Adventure Explorer package blends rugged mountain trekking, wildlife exploration in
tiger reserves, and immersive wild-lake camping. Experience exclusive experiences, enjoy nature-centric
stays, and walk away with absolute unforgettable memories in the unspoiled wilderness of Mizoram.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL & BASECAMP SETUP
Arrive in Aizawl, the capital of Mizoram. Private transfer to your basecamp hotel. Gear up for the adventure
ahead with a briefing session.
DAY 02 | REIEK MOUNTAIN TREKKING
Embark on an exhilarating trek to the summit of Reiek Mountain. Witness stunning panoramic views of the
Mizoram hills and untouched forest canopy.

DAY 03 | DAMPA TIGER RESERVE WILDLIFE SAFARI
Venture into the Dampa Tiger Reserve. Explore one of India’s most remote wildlife sanctuaries, spotting
diverse flora and rare wildlife in their natural habitat.
DAY 04 | PALAK LAKE ADVENTURE
Travel to Palak Lake, the largest lake in Mizoram. Enjoy boating and camping under the stars in the peaceful,
wild landscape.
DAY 05 | CAVING & WILDERNESS TRAIL
Explore hidden cave networks in the remote hills of Mizoram, experiencing an off-the-grid wilderness trek.
DAY 06 | CULTURAL IMMERSION & RELAXATION
Visit traditional Mizo villages, interacting with local communities and learning indigenous mountain survival
and craft techniques.
DAY 07 | DEPARTURE
Final breakfast before your TRAGUIN private transfer, completing your premium Mizoram adventure.
