TOUR OVERVIEW
Discover the untouched beauty and cultural sophistication of Mizoram with this TRAGUIN curated luxury
journey. Designed for those seeking comfort amidst wild nature, this Luxury Mizoram itinerary combines
premium accommodations, guided cultural insights, and breathtaking scenic explorations, guaranteeing
unforgettable memories in Northeast India's pristine hills.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL & LUXURY CHECK-IN
Arrive in Aizawl, private transfer to your premium hotel. Enjoy a curated welcome dinner with stunning
mountain vistas.
DAY 02 | CULTURE & HANDICRAFT EXPLORATION
Private guided tour of the Mizoram State Museum and traditional handicraft centers, exploring the local art
and iconic attractions.
