TOUR OVERVIEW
Embark on the ultimate journey through the hills, culture, and nature of Mizoram. This TRAGUIN curated
expedition covers the absolute best of the state, blending cultural heritage in Aizawl, stunning mountain
peaks, waterfalls, and serene lakes. Designed for families wanting the complete experience, this itinerary
creates unforgettable memories in the heart of Northeast India’s pristine landscape.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL & MOUNTAIN WELCOME
Arrive in Aizawl. Private transfer to your handpicked premium resort. Enjoy a quiet evening with panoramic
valley vistas.

DAY 02 | AIZAWL CULTURAL HERITAGE
Comprehensive tour of Aizawl's museums and heritage centers, discovering the history and iconic
attractions of Mizo society.
DAY 03 | REIEK MOUNTAIN & HERITAGE VILLAGE
Excursion to Reiek Mountain. Explore the heritage village and enjoy local traditions, offering a fantastic family
experience in the hills.
DAY 04 | VANTAWNG FALLS & NATURAL WONDERS
Witness the awe-inspiring Vantawng Falls and explore the surrounding lush valleys, capturing breathtaking
landscapes together.
DAY 05 | TAMDIL LAKE LEISURE
Enjoy a day of tranquility at Tamdil Lake. Boating, relaxation, and exploring the serene natural environment at
a family-friendly pace.
DAY 06 | PHAWNGPUI BLUE MOUNTAIN SOJOURN
Journey to Phawngpui, the Blue Mountain. Experience the scenic beauty of the highest peak, offering
panoramic views of the hills.
DAY 07 | CULTURAL GALA & FAREWELL
A final day for local exploration, interactive craft sessions, and a grand family farewell dinner gala, creating
unforgettable memories.
DAY 08 | DEPARTURE
Final breakfast before your TRAGUIN private transfer, completing your complete Mizoram family
expedition.
