TOUR OVERVIEW
Enjoy a comfortable, relaxed, and culturally enriching experience in the beautiful hills of Mizoram with this
TRAGUIN curated leisure itinerary. Specifically designed for senior citizens, this Mizoram Leisure Sojourn
prioritizes comfort, slow-paced sightseeing, and easy access to local culture, guaranteeing unforgettable
memories in the serene capital of Aizawl.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | COMFORTABLE ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL
Arrive in Aizawl. Your private chauffeur transfers you to a premium hotel with easy access and beautiful
panoramic views. Savor a relaxed evening.
DAY 02 | LIGHT CULTURAL EXPLORATION
A gentle city tour featuring the serene Mizoram State Museum and accessible local handicraft centers at a
relaxed, senior-friendly pace.

DAY 03 | HERITAGE & SCENIC LEISURE
A short, comfortable drive to heritage areas with minimal walking required, enjoying the cool mountain air and

DAY 04 | VALLEY VISTAS & RELAXED DINING
Spend a peaceful day overlooking the Aizawl valley from comfortable viewpoints, enjoying local delicacies in a
quiet, leisurely atmosphere.
DAY 05 | LOCAL MARKET & RELAXATION
A final day for light shopping and soaking in the tranquil ambiance of Aizawl, ensuring you collect
unforgettable memories at your own pace.
DAY 06 | COMFORTABLE DEPARTURE
Final luxury breakfast before your TRAGUIN private chauffeur transfers you comfortably to the airport for your
flight home.
