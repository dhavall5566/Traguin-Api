TOUR OVERVIEW
Conquer the heights of Mizoram’s most famous peak with our TRAGUIN curated Blue Mountain Trekking
Expedition. Designed for serious adventure seekers, this high-altitude itinerary pushes boundaries, offering a
rugged experience into the clouds of Northeast India’s pristine mountain wilderness, ensuring unforgettable
memories.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL & EXPEDITION BRIEFING
Arrive in Aizawl. Private transfer to your expedition basecamp. Gear check and detailed route briefing for your
Blue Mountain trek.

DAY 02 | TRANSIT TO MOUNTAIN BASE
Drive through stunning valleys to the base of Phawngpui (Blue Mountain). Camp in the wild, surrounded by
natural beauty.
DAY 03 | BLUE MOUNTAIN TREKKING COMMENCEMENT
Begin the ascent toward Phawngpui peak. Navigate through dense mountain forests and observe rare, high-
altitude flora and mountain wildlife.
DAY 04 | SUMMIT REACH & PANORAMIC CLOUD VISTAS
Conquer the summit of Phawngpui. Witness the breathtaking landscapes above the clouds, a truly
unmatched exclusive experience.
DAY 05 | DESCENT & WILDERNESS EXPLORATION
Descend from the peak with a guided exploration of remote ridge-line trails, capturing stunning photography
points in the wilderness.
DAY 06 | RETURN TO AIZAWL & CELEBRATION
Return to Aizawl. Reflect on your adventure during a final celebratory dinner party, creating unforgettable
memories.
DAY 07 | DEPARTURE
Final breakfast before your TRAGUIN private transfer to the airport, completing your premium adventure
expedition.
