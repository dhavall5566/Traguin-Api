TOUR OVERVIEW
Escape to the serene, mist-covered hills of Mizoram with this TRAGUIN curated family getaway. Designed to
offer a perfect mix of cultural discovery and peaceful mountain leisure, this Hills of Mizoram itinerary ensures
your family enjoys premium stays, comfortable exploration, and unforgettable memories in Northeast India’s
most scenic landscape.
THE IMMERSIVE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AIZAWL & MOUNTAIN WELCOME
Arrive in Aizawl, the capital of Mizoram. Private transfer to your handpicked premium resort. Enjoy a quiet
evening with spectacular panoramic views of the hills.
DAY 02 | AIZAWL CULTURAL DISCOVERY
Explore the cultural highlights of Aizawl at a family-friendly pace. Visit museums and local heritage centres,
learning about Mizo traditions and iconic attractions.
