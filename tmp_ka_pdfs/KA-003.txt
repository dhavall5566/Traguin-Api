DAY 04 KUSHALNAGAR SPIRITUAL EXPLORATION | GOLDEN TEMPLE & ELEPHANT
SANCTUARY
Enjoy breakfast before driving down to Kushalnagar to discover the diverse cultural colors of Coorg. Visit the
magnificent Namdroling Monastery, widely known as the Golden Temple, which houses breathtaking 40-foot
gilded Buddha statues and exquisite Tibetan frescoes. Soak in the spiritual serenity of this sacred space.
Afterward, take a romantic walk to the Dubare Elephant Camp along the banks of the Cauvery River. Here, you
can watch these gentle giants being bathed and fed. Return to your mountain sanctuary in the evening for a
special farewell experience curated uniquely for you.

SHOPPING, DINING & LOCAL CULTURE
Coorg Shopping Highlights: Explore the lively local markets in Madikeri to purchase freshly roasted coffee beans,
homemade fruit wines (like bird's eye chili or plum wine), pure wild honey, and aromatic local spices like green
cardamom and black pepper. 
Famous Local Food: We highly recommend visiting cozy local estate cafes to sample authentic Kodava cuisine,
featuring the famous *Kadamuttu* (steamed rice balls) served alongside rich, traditional local curries and fresh
handmade chocolates.
