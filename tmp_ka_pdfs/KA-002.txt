DESTINATION SPOTLIGHT & TRAVEL INSIGHTS
**Why Visit Hampi?** As the centerpiece of any premium Karnataka Tour Package, Hampi is an exceptional
destination that boasts a surreal topography of massive balancing granite boulders paired perfectly with intricately
carved stone palaces. It stands proudly as one of the top tourist places in Karnataka, providing an incredible blend
of historical grandeur, spiritual depth, and rustic charm. It is an extraordinary location for a Luxury Hampi Holiday,
an unforgettable Hampi Family Tour, or an artistic Hampi Honeymoon Package. 
**Top Tourist Places & Instagram Locations:** Capture breathtakingly scenic beauty and unforgettable memories at
the world-famous Stone Chariot within the Vittala Temple Complex, the monolithic Lakshmi Narasimha, the
stunning stepped tank, the elegant Lotus Mahal, and the royal stables. Watch an unforgettable sunset from the
heights of Hemakuta Hill or Malyavanta Hill, where the golden light bathes the entire landscape in a magical glow,
making it a highly searched landmark for popular travel platforms.

DAY 03 MYTHOLOGICAL KISHKINDHA TRAILS | SACRED CORACLE RIDE &
VIRUPAKSHA TEMPLE
Awaken early for a magical morning experience. Travel to the banks of the sacred Tungabhadra River for an
exclusive, custom coracle boat ride—a traditional circular vessel that floats gracefully past hidden riverbank
shrines and sheer granite gorges. Next, explore the grand Virupaksha Temple, an active place of worship
since the 7th century, featuring a magnificent gopuram. In the afternoon, cross the river to visit Anegundi, an
ancient village belonging to the mythical kingdom of Kishkindha from the epic Ramayana. Conclude your
day with a short walk up Malyavanta Hill to hear beautiful live chanting of ancient verses while taking in an
incredible panoramic view of the vast valley.
