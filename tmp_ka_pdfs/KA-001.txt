DESTINATION SPOTLIGHT & TRAVEL INSIGHTS
Why Visit Karnataka? This iconic pairing delivers the ultimate dual-world Indian holiday—combining an ultra-
luxury heritage immersion with an exquisite mountain retreat. Mysore, widely celebrated for its grand royal palaces,
captivates multi-generational families with its magnificent UNESCO-worthy architectural sites, rich silk weaving
heritage, and sandalwood fragrances. Coorg, often affectionately termed the "Scotland of India," complements this
grand vibe by providing a refreshing alpine sanctuary famous for its pristine natural ecosystems, sprawling organic
coffee estates, and panoramic viewpoints that dominate social media feeds. This makes it an exceptional choice for
a Karnataka Family Tour or a romantic Karnataka Honeymoon Package. 
Top Tourist Places & Instagram Locations: Capture striking family photographs at the monumental Mysore
Palace lit by a hundred thousand bulbs, the historic Chamundi Hills, the sprawling Brindavan Gardens, the dramatic
Abbey Falls, Raja’s Seat at sunset, and the spiritual Tibetan Golden Temple at Bylakuppe. This specialized
TRAGUIN Karnataka Packages layout guarantees that you experience these world-famous sightseeing marvels
completely away from the crowds, utilizing expertly timed access and private localized guidance for a true Luxury
Karnataka Holiday.

DAY 02 MYSORE SIGHTSEEING & TRANSFER TO COORG | JOURNEY TO
THE COFFEE KINGDOM
Savor a magnificent multi-cuisine breakfast before diving into the definitive Mysore sightseeing experience.
Visit the legendary, breathtaking Mysore Palace, an extraordinary blend of Indo-Saracenic architecture filled
with grand mahogany ceilings, stained glass windows, and solid silver doors. Next, drive up the historic
Chamundi Hills to visit the ancient Sri Chamundeshwari Temple and view the monolithic Nandi Bull

DAY 04 COORG SIGHTSEEING | WATERFALLS, FORTRESSES & SUNSET
PANORAMAS
Indulge in a rich breakfast spread before heading out for a comprehensive day of Coorg sightseeing. Visit
the Dubare Elephant Camp along the banks of the Kaveri River, where your family can experience an
interactive session watching elephants bathe and learn about conservation efforts. Next, visit the sparkling,
cascading waters of Abbey Falls nestled beautifully within spice gardens. Explore the historic Madikeri Fort,
which reflects a unique combination of European and Asian architecture. As late afternoon approaches, your
private guide will escort you to Raja’s Seat (The Seat of Kings), where rolling hills and mist-covered valleys
create a phenomenal panorama. Watch a spectacular sunset while capturing unforgettable memories.
