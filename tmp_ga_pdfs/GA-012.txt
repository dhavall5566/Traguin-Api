DAY 03 | SOUTH GOA HERITAGE & ROMANTIC LUXURY CRUISE
PORTUGUESE ARCHITECTURE & LUXURY PRIVATE YACHT EXPERIENCE
Today, travel south to uncover the rich cultural roots of old colonial Goa. Walk hand-in-hand through the

the Mandovi River.
