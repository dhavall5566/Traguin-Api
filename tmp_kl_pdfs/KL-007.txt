DAY 01 | COCHIN ARRIVAL TO MUNNAR
Your spectacular Kerala Family Tour and romantic holiday begins as you land at Cochin International
Airport. You will be warmly received with traditional hospitality by a specialized TRAGUIN representative.
Step into your premium luxury private vehicle and set forth on a visually stunning scenic route toward
Munnar—the crown jewel of South India's hill stations. 
As the road winds upwards, witness the dramatic landscape transformation from bustling coastal plains
into rolling layers of emerald hills. En route, make a stop at the breathtaking Cheeyappara and Valara
Waterfalls, where sheets of water tumble down rocky cliffs amid dense forests, offering the perfect
backdrop for your first couple photography points. Arrive in Munnar, breathe in the crisp, spice-scented
mountain air, and check into your handpicked ultra-luxury resort. Enjoy a private, hassle-free check-in
experience.

DAY 02 | MUNNAR SIGHTSEEING
Wake up to the gentle chirping of exotic birds and hills draped gracefully in morning fog. Today is
dedicated to an extensive, premium Munnar Sightseeing experience. After a lavish spread for

DAY 03 | MUNNAR TO THEKKADY
Bid a temporary farewell to the misty heights of Munnar as your Luxury Kerala Holiday progresses
down toward Thekkady (Periyar), India's premier spice and wildlife wonderland. The driving route is an
absolute feast for the eyes, characterized by sweeping views of deep valleys, dramatic ridges, and
sprawling commercial plantations of cardamom, pepper, and cinnamon. 
Upon entering Thekkady, check into your ultra-premium jungle resort that perfectly blends rustic
wilderness with sophisticated high-end luxury. In the afternoon, head out for an unforgettable boat cruise
across the scenic Periyar Lake. Keep your cameras primed at the photography points to capture herds
of wild elephants, bison, sambar deer, and rare waterbirds drinking along the water's edge. Later,
explore the vibrant local spice markets for premium souvenirs. 
Sightseeing Included: Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary,
Periyar Lake Cruise, Spice Plantation Tour.
Optional Activities: Elephant bathing experience,
guided jungle night trekking, Kathakali cultural
dance show.
Evening Experience: Exploring bustling local
markets followed by an authentic Keralan clay-pot
dining experience.
Overnight Stay: Elite Wilderness Resort in
Thekkady.
Meals Included: Breakfast & Dinner
DAY 04 | THEKKADY TO ALLEPPEY HOUSEBOAT
Prepare your senses for the absolute highlight of your Destination Honeymoon Package. After a
hearty breakfast, drive down the hills to Alleppey (Alappuzha), fondly referred to as the Venice of the
East. At noon, step aboard your private, ultra-luxury, handpicked traditional Kettuvallam (Houseboat),
floating gracefully on the pristine waters of the Vembanad Lake. 
As your private floating palace glides through intricate networks of canals, look out at vibrant green
paddy fields, towering coconut groves, and charming lakeside hamlets. Your dedicated on-board master
chef will prepare exquisite local culinary masterpieces using fresh local catch and organic farm produce.
Sip tender coconut water, recline on the sun deck, and watch one of the most spectacular, globally
acclaimed sunsets over the backwaters. This is the ultimate TRAGUIN Signature Experience.

Backwater Canals, Pathiramanal Island view.
Optional Activities: Traditional narrow-canal
village canoe ride, authentic Toddy shop visit.
Evening Experience: Star-lit peaceful night
anchorage over the calm, glass-like backwaters.

DAY 05 | ALLEPPEY TO COCHIN
Enjoy your morning coffee as the houseboat gently glides back toward the jetty. Disembark after a
memorable breakfast and travel back to the historic port city of Cochin. Known as the Queen of the
Arabian Sea, Cochin flawlessly fuses old-world colonial charm with ultra-modern coastal luxury. 
Check into your ultra-luxury heritage hotel in Fort Cochin or a contemporary waterfront property. Spend
your afternoon uncovering centuries of history. Visit the legendary Fort Cochin Beach and the iconic
Chinese Fishing Nets, which stand tall as monuments of ancient global maritime trade. Wander
through the artistic streets of Mattancherry, marvel at the murals inside the Dutch Palace, explore the
historic Jewish Synagogue, and stroll through Jew Town shopping for exquisite antiques and essential
oils. 
Sightseeing Included: Fort Cochin Chinese Fishing Nets, St.
Francis Church, Santa Cruz Basilica, Mattancherry Dutch Palace,
Jewish Synagogue & Jew Town.
Optional Activities: Luxury Arabian

seafood fine-dining.

global cafes along the Fort Cochin walkways.

DAY 06 | COCHIN DEPARTURE
Your magical, premium journey through paradise draws to a gentle close today. Savor your lavish spread
for breakfast at the hotel, reflecting upon the incredible sights, deep relaxation, and enchanting moments

SHOPPING & RECOMMENDATIONS
Kerala is a treasure grove for connoisseurs of fine lifestyle products. During your leisure time, we highly
recommend checking out these handpicked local gems: 
Sought-After Souvenirs: High-grade green cardamom, black pepper, organic tea leaves, aromatic essential
oils (Lemongrass/Eucalyptus), and authentic sandalwood handicrafts.
Handloom & Textiles: Purchase a beautiful, timeless Kasavu Saree (traditional white and gold silk garment) at
high-end boutiques in Cochin.
Gourmet Dining Spots: Sample fresh Karimeen Pollichathu (pearl spot fish in banana leaf), Malabar prawn
curry, and crispy banana chips fried fresh right before your eyes.
Instagram Vantage Spots: The beautiful infinity pools overlooking tea valleys in Munnar and the sunset
silhouettes over Fort Cochin's Chinese nets.
IMPORTANT TOUR NOTES & TRAVEL POLICIES
Hotel Check-in/Check-out: Standard check-in time across luxury resorts is 14:00 hrs and check-out is 11:00
hrs. Early arrivals are subject to room availability.
Houseboat Policies: Houseboat check-in time is strictly at 12:00 PM. Cruising is permitted until 5:30 PM due to
local fishing net regulations, after which the boat anchors securely in a scenic spot. AC works continuously in
Premium and Luxury tiers.
Eravikulam National Park: Please note the sanctuary remains temporarily closed for a short duration annually
(typically Feb-March) for the animal calving season. Alternative luxury sightseeing will be seamlessly arranged.
Hill Station Driving: For your absolute safety, driving through high-altitude misty mountain regions is restricted
during late-night hours. Daily sightseeing schedules must be well-coordinated.
Advance Booking Suggestion: Given the exceptionally high demand for the Best Munnar Luxury Couple
properties, securing your reservation 45–60 days in advance is highly recommended to guarantee premier room
tiers.
