DAY 01 | UDAIPUR ARRIVAL
ROYAL RECEPTION & PRIVATE LAKE PICHOLA SUNSET CRUISE
Your majestic journey begins as you step into the regal air of Udaipur. A private premium chauffeur from TRAGUIN
welcomes you at the airport or railway station with luxury amenities, refreshing cold towels, and a beautiful

DAY 02 | UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING
MONUMENTAL ROMANCE & THE GRAND CITY PALACE CHRONICLES
Savor a gourmet breakfast before embarking on an extensive Udaipur Sightseeing tour. Today is dedicated to
exploring the top tourist places in Udaipur with an expert heritage guide. Step inside the monumental City Palace,
the largest palace complex in Rajasthan, to marvel at brilliant mirror work, private royal courtyards, and ancient
artifacts. Next, stroll hand-in-hand through Saheliyon-ki-Bari (The Garden of the Maids), capturing stunning
photographs next to its marble fountains and lush lotus pools. In the afternoon, explore the grand Jagdish Temple,
a living architectural masterpiece of Indo-Aryan design, followed by a romantic walk through the old city markets.

DAY 03 | SAJJANGARH PALACE & CULTURE
THE MONSOON PALACE VISTA & IMMERSIVE MEWARI CULTURAL SHOW
Awaken to the soothing sounds of lakeside birds. Today, your TRAGUIN Packages itinerary takes you higher into
the hills to the famous Sajjangarh Palace, also known as the Monsoon Palace. Perched high on a peak of the
Aravalli range, it offers sweeping, breathtaking landscapes of Udaipur's entire lake system and countryside. In the
afternoon, enjoy an immersive experience at Bagore-ki-Haveli, located right on the waterfront. Here, you will
witness a vibrant, high-energy Rajasthani folk dance and puppet show that brings the rich history and heritage of
Rajasthan to life. End your night with a romantic candlelight dinner at an exclusive rooftop venue. 
Sightseeing Included
Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace, Biological Park, Bagore-ki-Haveli.
