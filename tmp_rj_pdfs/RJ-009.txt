BESPOKE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR
WELCOME TO THE PINK CITY – ROYAL GREETINGS & RELAXED EVENING
Your spectacular Premium Rajasthan Experience begins with a warm welcome. Upon arrival at Jaipur Airport, you
will receive a traditional royal greeting arranged by our dedicated TRAGUIN coordinators. Your private chauffeur will
assist with your luggage and drive you to your handpicked premium hotel in a comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle. 
The afternoon is reserved for your comfort, allowing you time to check in, unpack, and settle into your premium stays. In
the evening, enjoy a gentle, relaxed drive past the beautifully illuminated Albert Hall Museum, a great photography
point, followed by an elegant welcome dinner at a historic heritage restaurant. 
Sightseeing Included: Albert Hall illuminated drive, local
heritage walks.
Optional Activities: Visit to the local handicraft emporium.
Evening Experience: Relaxed sit-down traditional dinner.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur (Handpicked Premium Hotel).

DAY 02 | JAIPUR ROYAL EXPLORATION
PALACES OF MARVEL – COMFORTABLE FORT ASCENT & HISTORIC TEXTILES
Savor a delightful breakfast before embarking on a curated day of Jaipur Sightseeing. Your morning features a visit to
the magnificent Amer Fort, where we arrange comfortable vehicle access directly to the main courtyard to avoid steep
climbs. Explore the stunning, mirrored halls of the Sheesh Mahal while listening to captivating stories from an expert
local guide. 
In the afternoon, enjoy a photo stop at the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds) from a comfortable street-level view,
followed by an accessible golf-cart tour of the majestic City Palace complex. Witness royal costumes, historic weaponry,
and architectural courtyards designed with minimal walking steps. 
Sightseeing Included: Amer Fort Courtyard, Hawa Mahal
Photo Stop, City Palace via Golf-Cart.
Optional Activities: Private block-printing demonstration.
Evening Experience: Leisurely stroll through the accessible
gardens of Kanak Vrindavan.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur.
DAY 03 | JAIPUR TO PUSHKAR
SACRED WATERFRONTS – THE HOLY LAKE & REVERED BRAHMA TEMPLE
After a relaxed breakfast, take a smooth, short morning drive to the peaceful town of Pushkar, home to one of the world's
very few temples dedicated to Lord Brahma. Upon arrival, check into a beautiful garden resort selected for its calm
setting and single-level, easily accessible cottages. 
In the late afternoon, enjoy a gentle walk to the sacred Pushkar Lake and its holy ghats. Witness the peaceful evening
prayer ceremonies and take in the spiritual music echoing across the water. This scenic beauty offers a highly searched
tourism experience that leaves a lasting emotional connection. 
Sightseeing Included: Brahma Temple, Pushkar Holy Lake
Ghats, Rose Gardens.
Optional Activities: Relaxing traditional foot massage at the
resort spa.
Evening Experience: Spiritually uplifting sunset chants by
the lake.
Overnight Stay: Pushkar (Premium Heritage Resort).
DAY 04 | PUSHKAR TO JODHPUR
JOURNEY TO THE BLUE CITY – SUNSET FORT VIEWS & REFRESHING ROYALTY
Enjoy a leisurely morning breakfast before heading west toward Jodhpur, the magnificent "Blue City." The drive features
well-paved highways with pre-planned comfort stops at excellent roadside boutique hotels. Arrive in Jodhpur by mid-
afternoon and check into your handpicked premium accommodation, where modern luxury meets classic heritage
hospitality.

DAY 05 | JODHPUR SIGHTSEEING
CITADELS OF HERITAGE – MEHRANGARH FORT ELEVATOR ACCESS & MARBLE
CENOTAPHS
Today is dedicated to a relaxed tour of Jodhpur Sightseeing. Visit Mehrangarh Fort, where we arrange exclusive lift and
elevator access to the top museum levels, completely bypassing the steep stone slopes. Admire the exquisite collection of
royal palanquins, ancient furniture, and historic miniatures. 
Next, visit Jaswant Thada, a serene white marble memorial set amidst peaceful lakeside gardens, offering easy, level
paths to walk. Conclude your afternoon with a smooth drive past the grand Umaid Bhawan Palace, one of the world's
largest private residences, and explore its fascinating vintage car museum. 
Sightseeing Included: Mehrangarh Fort with Elevator,
Jaswant Thada Gardens, Umaid Bhawan Car Collection.
Optional Activities: Soft handicraft shopping at Sadar bazaar
with private pickup.
Evening Experience: Fine-dining dinner featuring signature
Rajasthani delicacies.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur.
DAY 06 | JODHPUR TO UDAIPUR
THE VENICE OF THE EAST – EN ROUTE RANAKPUR & SUNSET LAKESIDE CRUISE
Travel south toward Udaipur through a scenic route along the rolling Aravali Hills. Stop along the way to visit the
architectural masterpiece of Ranakpur Jain Temple, famous for its magnificent marble pillars. The temple offers a highly
spiritual and peaceful atmosphere, perfect for a mid-journey break. 
Arrive in Udaipur, the "City of Lakes," by late afternoon. Experience an enchanting, private boat cruise on Lake Pichola,
where you can watch the sunset behind the beautiful Jag Mandir and the grand City Palace. It's a truly memorable
experience that highlights why this is considered the best time to visit Rajasthan. 
Sightseeing Included: Ranakpur Temple, Lake Pichola
Private Cruise, Jag Mandir Views.
Optional Activities: Photography session during sunset.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur (Premium Lakeside Resort).

ROYAL GARDENS & FAREWELL – MEMORIES BEYOND DESTINATIONS
On your final morning of Udaipur Sightseeing, explore Saheliyon-ki-Bari (the Garden of Maidens), a beautiful, flat
park filled with marble fountains, lotus pools, and lush green lawns that are easy to walk through. 
Afterward, enjoy a final lunch with your family before your private chauffeur transfers you to Udaipur Airport for your
journey home. Your premium holiday concludes seamlessly, leaving you with beautiful, lifelong memories curated by
TRAGUIN.
