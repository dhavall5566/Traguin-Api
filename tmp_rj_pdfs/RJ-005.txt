DAY 02 | UDAIPUR EXPLORATION
ROYAL GRANDEUR – ARCHITECTURAL MARVELS & COUTURIER MARKETS
Savor a delightful morning breakfast before embarking on an extensive, curated tour of the legendary City Palace
complex, the largest palace structure in Rajasthan. Marvel at the intricate mirrored mosaics, opulent courtyards, and
priceless historic artifacts. Next, seek blessings at the 17th-century Jagdish Temple, an exquisite example of Indo-Aryan
architecture, before taking a peaceful walk through Saheliyon-ki-Bari (the Garden of Maidens), filled with beautiful lotus
pools and marble fountains. 
In the evening, discover the traditional art of Mewari miniature paintings during an interactive, private session with a
master artist. Later, enjoy a vibrant local shopping walk through Hathipole Bazaar to find exclusive handcrafted
souvenirs. 
Sightseeing Included: City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon-ki-
Bari, Vintage Car Museum.
Optional Activities: Cable Car Ride to Mansapurna
Karni Mata Temple.

Overnight Stay: Udaipur Palace Resort.
DAY 03 | UDAIPUR EXCURSION
MONSOON PALACE VISTAS & ROMANTIC LAKESIDE SERENITY
Begin your day with a scenic, winding drive up the Bansdara mountain to visit the historic Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace.
Perched high above the city, it offers sweeping, panoramic vistas of Udaipur's lakes and countryside. Afterward, enjoy a
peaceful afternoon near the quiet shores of Fateh Sagar Lake. Take a boat ride to Nehru Park or relax at a boutique café
overlooking the water. This day is specifically designed to let you slow down, enjoy each other's company, and capture
stunning photographs at top Instagram locations. 
Sightseeing Included: Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace, Fateh Sagar Circuit,

DAY 05 | JODHPUR SIGHTSEEING
CITADELS OF BLUE – THE MEHRANGARH FORT & ROYAL CENOTAPHS
Dedicate your day to exploring the best of Jodhpur Sightseeing. Visit the mighty Mehrangarh Fort, which stands 400
feet above the city. Explore its grand palaces, beautifully preserved museums, and ancient cannon ramparts that offer
stunning views of Jodhpur's iconic blue houses. Next, visit Jaswant Thada, a graceful white marble cenotaph built by the
lakeshore, often called the "Taj Mahal of Marwar." 
In the afternoon, enjoy a private, guided walk through the old city, starting from the iconic Clock Tower (Ghanta Ghar)
and navigating the colorful Sadar Market. Discover artisan spices, fine textiled handlooms, and exquisite local
handicrafts. 
Sightseeing Included: Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, Clock Tower,
Umaid Bhawan Museum.

Mehrangarh Fort moats.

LOCAL EXPERIENCES & SHOPPING INSIGHTS
Udaipur Shopping: Explore Shilpgram crafts village for traditional Rajasthani items, or visit Bada Bazar for authentic
silver jewelry and bandhani fabrics. Enjoy a sunset coffee at micro-cafés overlooking Ambrai Ghat. 
Jodhpur Shopping: Find world-famous Mehrangarh-inspired antiques, block-printed textiles, and beautiful leather
footwear at Kapraa Bazaar. Don't miss trying local delicacies like Mawa Kachori and Mirchi Vada at Jodhpur's historic
eateries.
