DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR
WELCOME TO THE PINK CITY – ROYAL COCKTAILS & HIGH-TEA WELCOME
Your extraordinary journey begins as you land in the grand capital city of Jaipur. Upon arrival, receive a stunning
traditional greeting featuring rose petals and refreshing mocktails, arranged under the careful guidance of TRAGUIN
concierge representatives. Relax as you are transferred to your luxury heritage hotel in a premium vehicle. 
Spend your afternoon settling into your premium stays. In the evening, gather for a signature TRAGUIN royal welcome
high-tea at a beautifully restored haveli café. This is the perfect opportunity to meet your fellow travelers, toast to the
upcoming journey, and take stunning group photographs at your first popular Instagram location.

DAY 02 | JAIPUR PALACE EXPLORATION
FORTRESS SPLENDOR & BLOCK PRINTING ARTISANSHIP
Savor a lavish buffet breakfast before exploring the magnificent Jaipur Sightseeing track. Your first stop is the stunning
Amer Fort, where you will explore the mirror-lined halls of the Sheesh Mahal. On your way back, stop to capture
gorgeous photographs outside the iconic Jal Mahal, a beautiful palace resting in the middle of Man Sagar Lake. 
In the afternoon, enjoy an exclusive hands-on workshop in the artisan village of Anokhi. Learn the delicate, centuries-old
art of hand-block printing from local women artisans, and create your own customized scarf as a beautiful souvenir of
your trip. 
Sightseeing Included: Amer Fort, Jal Mahal, Hawa Mahal
photography bay.
Optional Activities: Professional royal styling and saree
draping experience.
Evening Experience: Private dining experience featuring authentic
local Rajasthani delicacies.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur Luxury Heritage Hotel.
DAY 03 | JAIPUR'S SECRET GEMS & BAZAARS
INSTAGRAMMABLE PALACES & HIGH-END JEWELRY SHOPPING
Begin your morning at the stunning Patrika Gate, a masterpiece of colorful Rajasthani murals and one of the country's
top Instagram locations. Afterward, take a private guided tour of the City Palace, including access to the exclusive royal
rooms. Next, marvel at the ancient astronomical instruments at the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Jantar Mantar. 
Spend your afternoon exploring Jaipur's world-famous luxury bazaars. Accompanied by a shopping assistant, visit Johari
Bazar to browse fine gemstone jewelry, and explore Bapu Bazar for premium block-printed linens, dynamic footwear,
and authentic local handicrafts. 
Sightseeing Included: Patrika Gate, City Palace Museum, Jantar
Mantar.
Optional Activities: Private jewelry curation consult with
local experts.
Evening Experience: Sunset cocktails at a trendy rooftop café
overlooking the city.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur Luxury Heritage Hotel.

DAY 05 | PUSHKAR TO JODHPUR
THE BLUE CITY BASTIONS & BOUTIQUE STEPWELL CAFES
Enjoy an early morning yoga session on the resort lawns before traveling toward the striking city of Jodhpur. Arrive by
the afternoon and check into your boutique heritage hotel, located in the heart of the historic old town. 
Begin your exploration of Jodhpur Sightseeing at the breathtaking Mehrangarh Fort, exploring its ancient royal palaces
and taking in the panoramic views of the blue-painted houses below. Afterward, visit Jaswant Thada, a stunning white
marble cenotaph set next to a peaceful lake. Conclude your day with a relaxing coffee at a beautifully restored, historic
stepwell café. 
Sightseeing Included: Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, Toorji Ka
Jhalra Stepwell.
Optional Activities: Zip-lining over the majestic fort
battlements.
Evening Experience: A special farewell dinner to celebrate an
unforgettable trip.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur (Premium Boutique Heritage
Stay).
DAY 06 | JODHPUR DEPARTURE
FAREWELL ROYAL SISTERS – UNFORGETTABLE MEMORIES

BOUTIQUE SHOPPING & LOCAL FOOD RECOMMENDATIONS
Jaipur Bazaars: Look for beautiful block-printed clothing, authentic blue pottery pieces, and unique silver jewelry.
Enjoy a peaceful lunch at the historic, beautifully decorated Café Palladio. 
Pushkar Souvenirs: Find lovely rose waters, organic essential oils, and hand-woven canvas bags. Try the famous local
Malpua sweet near the lake ghats. 
Jodhpur Insights: Discover incredible local spices at the old Clock Tower market, along with traditional embroidered
leather footwear. Try the rich, local Mawa Kachori for a delicious taste of the city. 
IMPORTANT NOTES & TRAVEL INSIGHTS
Hotel check-in times generally begin at 14:00 hrs, and check-out is at 11:00 hrs.
Please pack modest clothing (such as scarves or long skirts) out of respect for local customs when visiting temples or
sacred sites.
The best time to visit Rajasthan's desert regions is from October through March when the daytime weather is
beautifully pleasant.
We recommend booking any optional tours or activities early to ensure availability during peak travel seasons.
