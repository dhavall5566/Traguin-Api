DAY 02 | JAIPUR
FORTRESSES OF GRANDEUR & ARCHITECTURAL WONDERS
Savor a lavish breakfast before embarking on a comprehensive day of exploration. Ascend to the
magnificent Amber Fort, a breathtaking masterclass in Rajput architecture. Your local specialist will guide
you through the Sheesh Mahal (Palace of Mirrors). Later, stop at the instantly recognizable Hawa Mahal
(Palace of Winds) for iconic family portraits. Conclude your daytime sightseeing at the grand City Palace
and the ancient astronomical observatory, Jantar Mantar. 
Sightseeing Included:
Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Jal Mahal.
Optional Activities:
Hot air balloon ride over Amber Fort at sunrise (highly recommended for families).
Overnight Stay &
Meals:
Premium Luxury Hotel, Jaipur | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner.
DAY 03 | JAIPUR TO JODHPUR
JOURNEY TO THE BLUE CITY & MEHRANGARH SUNSET VISUALS
Embark on a scenic drive across breathtaking landscapes toward the majestic Blue City of Jodhpur. Upon
check-in at your premium stay, prepare to visit one of India's largest and most imposing structures—the
MehrangarH Fort. Standing guard over the cobalt-blue houses below, it offers an immersive experience into
tales of medieval chivalry. Walk down to Jaswant Thada, a serene white marble cenotaph nestled by a
peaceful lake, perfect for evening contemplation. 
Sightseeing Included:
Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, Clock Tower Market.
Evening Experience:
Panoramic tea service overlooking the blue cityscape from a boutique terrace cafe.
Overnight Stay &
Meals:
Handpicked Luxury Heritage Hotel, Jodhpur | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner.

DAY 04 | JODHPUR TO JAISALMER
INTO THE THAR DESERT & GOLDEN OASIS ARRIVAL
Travel deep into the heart of the Thar Desert toward Jaisalmer, famously known as the Golden City. The
route unveils scenic rustic beauty and unique wildlife sightings. As you arrive, watch the golden sandstone
architecture glow under the afternoon sun. Check into your ultra-luxury resort and unwind. In the evening,
explore the vibrant local markets filled with unique camel-leather crafts, silver jewelry, and historic
keepsakes. 
Sightseeing Included:
Scenic overland desert route, Gadisar Lake twilight views.
Food Suggestion:
Try rich local delicacies like Ker Sangri and desert-inspired specialities.
Overnight Stay &
Meals:
Premium Luxury Oasis Resort, Jaisalmer | Meals: Breakfast & Dinner.
DAY 05 | JAISALMER
THE LIVING FORTRESS & EXCLUSIVE SUNSET SAM DUNES SAFARI
Discover the iconic Jaisalmer Fort (Sonay ka Qila), a living fortress where thousands still reside within its
ancient ramparts. Walk through the intricately carved Patwon ki Haveli and Salim Singh ki Haveli. In the
afternoon, transfer to the spectacular Sam Sand Dunes for an exclusive TRAGUIN signature highlight: a
private camel safari and luxury 4x4 dune bashing adventure. End the day under a star-lit desert sky
accompanied by authentic folk music and dance performances. 
Sightseeing Included:
Jaisalmer Living Fort, Patwon ki Haveli, Sam Sand Dunes.
Curated Experience:
VVIP desert camp setup with live cultural fire dance and premium stargazing.
Overnight Stay &
Meals:
Luxury Desert Camp / Resort, Sam Dunes | Meals: Breakfast & Royal Desert
Dinner.

DEPARTURE TO THE ROMANTIC CITY OF LAKES

DAY 07 | UDAIPUR
CITY PALACE MAJESTY & EXCLUSIVE PRIVATE BOAT CRUISE
Dedicate your day to discovering the wonders of Udaipur. Tour the massive City Palace complex, which
showcases detailed mirrors, murals, and historical artifacts. Visit Saheliyon-ki-Bari, the beautiful Garden of
the Maids of Honor, famous for its unique fountains and marble pavilions. Later, embark on an exclusive
private boat cruise across Lake Pichola, watching the sun dip below the shimmering waters behind Taj Lake
Palace. 
Sightseeing Included:
Udaipur City Palace, Saheliyon-ki-Bari, Jagdish Temple.
Photography Points:
Ambrai Ghat and City Palace balconies present unmatched royal backdrops.

Ultra-Luxury Lakeside Resort, Udaipur | Meals: Breakfast & Grand Finale Dinner.
DAY 08 | UDAIPUR
FAREWELL TO THE LAND OF ROYALTY
Enjoy a leisurely breakfast overlooking the calm waters of the lake. Take this morning for last-minute
souvenir shopping for traditional artwork, silver items, and authentic Mewari textiles. Your luxury vehicle will
then provide a smooth transfer to the Udaipur Airport or Railway Station, bringing your memorable
TRAGUIN Rajasthan Family Tour to a grand and seamless conclusion. 
Activities Included:
Morning souvenir shopping, scheduled luxury airport/station transfer.
