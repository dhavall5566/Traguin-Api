BESPOKE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR
WELCOME TO THE PINK CITY – ROYAL GREETINGS & EVENING AT CHOKHI DHANI
Your spectacular journey begins with a traditional royal welcome at Jaipur Airport, meticulously coordinated by our on-
ground luxury guest concierge. A private chauffeur will transport you to your magnificent palace hotel. In the evening,
immerse yourself in traditional culture at Chokhi Dhani, a curated ethnic village resort featuring folk dances, puppet
shows, and an authentic, multi-course Rajasthani feast. 
Sightseeing Included: Royal Welcome, Chauffeured Hotel
Transfer, Ethnic Cultural Village.
Meals Included: Traditional Gourmet Welcome Dinner.
Evening Experience: Cultural dance viewings & pottery
workshops.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur (Handpicked Luxury Palace).

DAY 02 | JAIPUR FULL DAY SIGHTSEEING
PALACES AND FORTS – AMBER FORT ELEPHANT RIDE & THE ICONIC HAWA MAHAL
Dedicate your day to a comprehensive Jaipur Sightseeing tour. Begin with an exclusive morning elephant ride or a jeep
ride to the gates of the hillside Amber Fort. Marvel at the exquisite Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace). Later, visit the striking
Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), the sprawling City Palace complex, and the historic Jantar Mantar observatory. 
Sightseeing Included: Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City
Palace, Jantar Mantar.
Optional Activities: Hot Air Balloon ride over the historic
fortresses.
Evening Experience: Shopping walk for precious gems at
Johari Bazaar.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur Luxury Palace.
DAY 03 | JAIPUR TO JODHPUR
JOURNEY TO THE SUN CITY – SCENIC HIGHWAYS & SUNSET OVER THE FORTRESS
Travel in premium comfort from Jaipur to the historic city of Jodhpur. Enjoy a beautiful drive past rural villages and
scenic landscapes. Upon arrival in Jodhpur, check into your premium heritage hotel. Spend the late afternoon relaxing or
watching the golden sunset look over the majestic blue horizons of the historic city. 
Sightseeing Included: Intercity scenic drive, blue city old
quarter view.
Meals Included: Premium Buffet Breakfast.
Evening Experience: Relaxed evening walk inside old town
streets.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur (Premium Heritage Hotel).
DAY 04 | JODHPUR SIGHTSEEING
CITADELS OF HERITAGE – MEHRANGARH FORT & WHITE MARBLE CENOTAPHS
Experience a detailed day of Jodhpur Sightseeing. Explore the majestic Mehrangarh Fort, which stands 400 feet above
the valley floor. Tour its grand museums, weapon collections, and historic courtyards. Next, visit Jaswant Thada, a
peaceful white marble cenotaph built by the lakeside, offering beautiful photography points. 
Sightseeing Included: Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada,
Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum.
Optional Activities: Flying Fox Zip-lining across fort
battlements.
Evening Experience: Fine dining at a rooftop restaurant
facing the fort.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur Premium Hotel.

DAY 05 | JODHPUR TO JAISALMER
INTO THE GREAT THAR DESERT – CAMEL SAFARI & LUXURY DESERT CAMPING
Drive deeper west toward the golden sands of Jaisalmer. Arrive at the Sam Sand Dunes in the afternoon, where you will
experience an authentic camel safari during sunset. Stay overnight in an ultra-luxury desert camp, featuring a premium
tented stay, live traditional folk music, and dining under a starry desert sky. 
Sightseeing Included: Thar Desert dunes, Sunset Camel
Trek.
Optional Activities: Dune-bashing in a private 4x4 vehicle.
Evening Experience: Live folk music performance and
stargazing.
Overnight Stay: Luxury Tented Desert Camp.
DAY 06 | JAISALMER FORT EXPLORATION TO UDAIPUR
THE LIVING FORTRESS & EMBARKING TOWARD THE REGAL LAKES
Explore the majestic Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Qila), one of the world's few fully inhabited medieval fortresses. Walk
through its narrow lanes and see the beautifully carved Patwon-ki-Haveli and Salim Singh-ki-Haveli. Later, enjoy a
smooth premium road transfer or convenient connectivity to the romantic city of Udaipur. 
Sightseeing Included: Golden Jaisalmer Fort, Historic
Havelis, Gadisar Lake.
Meals Included: Premium Breakfast.
Evening Experience: Relaxing late evening arrival check-in
at Udaipur.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur (Premium Lakefront Resort).
DAY 07 | UDAIPUR CITY PALACE & LAKES
VENICE OF THE EAST – LAKE PICHOLA CRUISE & THE RESPLENDENT PALACES
Enjoy an immersive day of Udaipur Sightseeing. Visit the magnificent City Palace complex by Lake Pichola,
discovering its hidden corridors, mirrored alcoves, and private collection galleries. In the late afternoon, enjoy a private,
scenic boat cruise out to Jag Mandir Palace, soaking in the beautiful lake views. 
Sightseeing Included: City Palace, Lake Pichola Cruise,
Jagdish Temple.
Optional Activities: Crystal Gallery private tour.
Evening Experience: Traditional dance show at Bagore-ki-
Haveli.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur Lakefront Resort.

DAY 08 | UDAIPUR ROYAL GARDENS & MONSOON PALACE
MAIDENS' FOUNTAINS & MONSOON VIEWS OVER THE HILLS
Visit Saheliyon-ki-Bari, a historic garden featuring beautiful lotus ponds, marble fountains, and carved pavilions. In the
afternoon, enjoy a scenic drive up to the hilltop Sajjangarh Monsoon Palace, which offers spectacular panoramic views
of Udaipur's surrounding lakes and the green Aravali hills. 
Sightseeing Included: Saheliyon-ki-Bari, Sajjangarh
Monsoon Palace, Fateh Sagar Lake.
Optional Activities: Speedboating on Fateh Sagar Lake.
Evening Experience: Farewell lakeside gala dinner.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur Lakefront Resort.
DAY 09 | UDAIPUR DEPARTURE
ROYAL FAREWELL – HOMEWARD BOUND WITH UNFORGETTABLE MEMORIES
Enjoy a final luxury breakfast at your resort before checking out. Spend your morning shopping for authentic souvenirs,
silver jewelry, or block-printed fabrics at local markets. Your premium holiday concludes with a private transfer to
Udaipur Airport for your journey home, leaving you with beautiful memories curated by TRAGUIN. 
Sightseeing Included: Airport departure transfer.
Meals Included: Premium Buffet Breakfast.
