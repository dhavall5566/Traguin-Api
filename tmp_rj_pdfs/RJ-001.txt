WHY CHOOSE THE PREMIUM RAJASTHAN FAMILY TOUR?
A Luxury Rajasthan Holiday stands out as India’s ultimate cultural retreat. Recognized globally for its opulent
palaces, vibrant bazaars, and legendary chivalry, it is the perfect backdrop for multi-generational bonding. When
choosing a Rajasthan Honeymoon Package or a multi-day family retreat, travelers seek a harmonious mix of
historical education, thrilling architectural sights, and indulgent hospitality.
Famous Attractions Covered: The iconic Amber Fort, City Palace Jaipur, Hawa Mahal, the towering Mehrangarh
Fort in Jodhpur, Jaswant Thada, the ethereal Lake Pichola, and the beautiful Saheliyon-ki-Bari in Udaipur. 
Most Searched & Popular Instagram Locations: The intricate facade of Patrika Gate, the stepwell of Toorji Ka
Jhalra, the stunning symmetrical arches of Amber Fort, and private sunset boat cruises on the waters of Lake
Pichola. This itinerary seamlessly blends classic heritage with contemporary luxury, establishing it as the most
sought-after TRAGUIN Rajasthan Package.

DAY 02 | JAIPUR SIGHTSEEING
THE ARCHITECTURAL MARVELS – FORTRESSES, PALACES & VIBRANT BAZAARS
Awake to a majestic sunrise and indulge in a lavish breakfast. Today features an immersive Jaipur Sightseeing
journey. Ascend to the hilltop grandeur of the Amber Fort, a breathtaking landscape of red sandstone and marble
overlooking Maota Lake. Explore the legendary Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace) accompanied by a private expert
storyteller. Post-lunch, discover the magnificent City Palace Jaipur, a living royal residence showing an impressive
collection of textiles and artifacts. Marvel at the astronomical brilliance of Jantar Mantar and stop for a flawless
photo-op in front of the iconic honeycomb structure of Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). Conclude the day with a
curated luxury shopping stroll through Johari Bazaar, famous for hand-cut gemstones and traditional textiles.

DAY 03 | JAIPUR TO JODHPUR
JOURNEY TO THE BLUE CITY – SUNSET AT THE IMPERIAL MEHRANGARH FORT
Bid farewell to Jaipur as your luxury vehicle glides smoothly toward Jodhpur, the legendary Blue City of India.
Check into your heritage property where old-world charm meets modern premium luxury. In the late afternoon,
approach the dramatic clifftop citadel of Mehrangarh Fort, one of the largest and most brilliantly preserved
fortresses in India. From its elevated ramparts, behold the breathtaking sprawling views of Jodhpur's signature
indigo-washed houses. Visit the nearby Jaswant Thada, an elegant cenotaph built of intricately carved, translucent
sheets of pure white marble. End your day enjoying a refreshing royal beverage while watching the sun dip below
the desert horizon.

DAY 04 | JODHPUR TO UDAIPUR
TRANSIT TO THE VENICE OF THE EAST – EN ROUTE RANAKPUR JAIN TEMPLE
After breakfast, traverse beautiful scenic landscapes as you travel south toward Udaipur, widely known as the
Venice of the East. Your private transfer features a curated stop at the masterfully engineered Ranakpur Jain
Temple, famous for its 1,444 uniquely carved marble pillars that change hue with the shifting sunlight. Continue
onward to Udaipur, arriving just as the afternoon light turns golden over the Aravalli hills. Check into your ultra-
luxury lakefront resort. Unwind completely before spending your evening at leisure, relaxing along the serene
waters of a city built on romance and legacy.

DAY 05 | UDAIPUR SIGHTSEEING
THE CITY OF LAKES – LAKESIDE SPLENDOR, GRAND PALACES & SUNSET CRUISE
Dedicate your day to discovering the breathtaking jewels of Udaipur. Begin with the majestic City Palace Udaipur,
Rajasthan's largest palace complex, displaying brilliant fusion architecture, shimmering mosaics, and historical royal
treasures. Next, visit the nearby Jagdish Temple and the lush gardens of Saheliyon-ki-Bari, adorned with marble
fountains, lotus pools, and elephant statues. The absolute pinnacle of this Premium Udaipur Experience arrives in
the late afternoon: an exclusive private boat cruise across the pristine, calm waters of Lake Pichola, sailing past
the legendary Lake Palace and Jag Mandir as the sun casts a magnificent golden glow over the palaces.
