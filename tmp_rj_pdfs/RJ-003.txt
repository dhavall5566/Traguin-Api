WHY CHOOSE THE BEST RAJASTHAN TOUR PACKAGE?
Rajasthan stands unmatched as India's premier luxury vacation capital, blending legendary hospitality with
deeply moving history. A Rajasthan Family Tour promises an enriching escape where kids and grandparents
alike can marvel at massive architectural wonders, discover vintage car collections, and dive into vibrant local
bazaars. 
Our most searched experiences highlight the top tourist places in Rajasthan, covering the breathtaking

DAY-WISE IMMERSIVE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN JODHPUR
WELCOME TO THE BLUE CITY & REGAL RECEPTION 
Your highly anticipated Luxury Rajasthan Holiday begins the exact moment you land in Jodhpur. A
professional representative from TRAGUIN will extend a traditional royal greeting at the airport or railway
station before chauffeuring your family to a handpicked premium heritage hotel. After a smooth, priority check-
in, take some time to relax in your luxurious surroundings. In the afternoon, set out for your first look at
Jodhpur sightseeing. Visit the serene Jaswant Thada, a majestic white marble cenotaph that reflects
beautifully over a peaceful lake. Later, explore the bustling local markets around the historic Clock Tower,
where you can sample iconic local delicacies and shop for rich textiles. 
Sightseeing Included:
Jaswant Thada, Ghanta Ghar (Clock Tower) & Local Bazaars.
Evening Experience:
Leisured walk through traditional Spice Markets and heritage lanes.
Overnight Stay:
Premium Luxury Hotel / Heritage Resort in Jodhpur.
Meals Included:
Dinner.
DAY 02 | JODHPUR SIGHTSEEING
EXPLORING THE MIGHTY MEHRANGARH & ROYAL LEGACIES 
Savor a lavish buffet breakfast before dedicating your day to the top tourist places in Jodhpur. Stand in awe
beneath the towering Mehrangarh Fort, one of India's largest and most magnificent structures, rising vertically
above the city. An expert local guide will lead your family through beautifully preserved palaces, historic
courtyards, and a museum boasting a collection of royal palanquins and armor. Capture stunning panoramic
family photos from the fort ramparts, overlooking an ocean of indigo-blue houses. Next, visit the spectacular
Umaid Bhawan Palace, a stunning golden-yellow sandstone monument that serves as both a luxury hotel and
the private residence of the current Maharaja. 
Sightseeing Included:
Mehrangarh Fort, Sheesh Mahal, Phool Mahal, Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum.
Optional Activities:
Thrilling Flying Fox (Zipline) adventure over the battlements of Mehrangarh Fort.
Evening Experience:
An exclusive rooftop dinner overlooking the beautifully illuminated Mehrangarh Fort.
Overnight Stay:
Premium Luxury Hotel / Heritage Resort in Jodhpur.
Meals Included:
Breakfast & Dinner.

DAY 03 | JODHPUR TO JAISALMER DESERT
JOURNEY TO THE GOLDEN SAND DUNES OF THAR 
Today, your TRAGUIN Desert Discovery ventures deep into the heart of the Thar Desert. Check out after
breakfast for a scenic road trip to Jaisalmer. Watch the landscape slowly transform into vast stretches of
golden sands and charming rustic villages. By afternoon, arrive at an exclusive luxury tented desert camp
nestled within the famous Sam Sand Dunes. Enjoy a traditional welcome with folk music, tikka, and flower
garlands. As the sun begins to lower, embark on an iconic camel safari across the rolling sand dunes, or
choose an optional high-octane 4x4 Jeep dune bashing experience. Conclude your evening at the camp with
an immersive cultural program featuring captivating Rajasthani folk dances (Kalbelia) and a sumptuous
traditional dinner under a starry night sky. 
Sightseeing Included:
Scenic drive through the Thar Desert, traditional rural desert vistas.
Optional Activities:
Thrilling 4x4 Jeep Safari, Quad Biking, or Paramotoring over dunes.
Evening Experience:
Sunset camel ride, live folk dance, cultural music, and stargazing.
Overnight Stay:
Premium Luxury Desert Camp (AC Swiss Tents) at Sam Sand Dunes.
Meals Included:
Breakfast & Dinner.
DAY 04 | JAISALMER DESERT TO JAISALMER CITY
THE LIVING FORT & ARCHITECTURAL MARVELS OF THE GOLDEN CITY 
Wake up early to catch a breathtaking desert sunrise over the expansive dunes before enjoying an authentic
breakfast. Check out from your camp and travel a short distance to Jaisalmer City. Check into a handpicked
premium hotel before setting out to explore the crown jewel of your Jaisalmer Sightseeing tour: the majestic
Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Qila). Known as India's only major living fort, nearly a quarter of the old city's population
still resides within its walls. Wander through winding golden sandstone alleyways lined with unique shops,
ancient Jain temples, and ornate palaces. Later, visit the spectacular Patwon ki Haveli and Nathmal ki Haveli
—architectural masterpieces adorned with unbelievably intricate stone carving work. 
Sightseeing Included:
Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon ki Haveli, Nathmal ki Haveli, Salim Singh ki Haveli.
Evening Experience:
A scenic evening visit to Gadisar Lake, a peaceful rainwater lake surrounded by
shrines.
Overnight Stay:
Premium Luxury Hotel / Palace Heritage Property in Jaisalmer.
Meals Included:
Breakfast & Dinner.

DAY 05 | JAISALMER HERITAGE EXPLORATION
HAUNTED LEGENDS & IMPERIAL CENOTAPHS 
Dedicate your fifth day to exploring the fascinating historical and cultural layers of Jaisalmer. Following a
delicious breakfast, visit Bada Bagh to view a collection of beautiful royal cenotaphs that glows wonderfully in
the morning sun. Next, journey to the intriguing abandoned village of Kuldhara. According to local lore, this
ancient Paliwal Brahmin settlement was deserted overnight centuries ago, leaving behind an exceptionally
well-preserved ghost town full of mysteries. Return to the city for an afternoon of leisure, allowing your family
to shop for famous Jaisalmer souvenirs like leather goods, yellow sandstone carvings, embroidery items, and
traditional jewelry. 
Sightseeing Included:
Bada Bagh Cenotaphs, Kuldhara Ghost Village, Vyas Chhatri.
Evening Experience:
A relaxed sunset view at Vyas Chhatri, dedicated to the sage Vyasa.
Overnight Stay:
Premium Luxury Hotel / Palace Heritage Property in Jaisalmer.
Meals Included:
Breakfast & Dinner.
DAY 06 | DEPARTURE FROM JAISALMER / JODHPUR
BIDDING FAREWELL TO MARWAR 
Savor your final breakfast of this unforgettable Premium Rajasthan Experience. Depending on your flight or
train arrangements, your private chauffeur will transport you comfortably to Jaisalmer Airport/Railway Station
or drive you back to Jodhpur Airport for your onward journey. Head home with cherished, lifelong family
memories of the magnificent desert dunes and grand palaces, all brought to life through the exceptional care
and expertise of TRAGUIN. 
Transfers Included:
Private drop-off at Jaisalmer or Jodhpur Airport / Railway Station.
Meals Included:
Breakfast.
