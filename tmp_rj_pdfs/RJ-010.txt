WHY CHOOSE A PREMIUM FAMILY TOUR IN RAJASTHAN?
Planning a Rajasthan Family Tour means stepping directly into India's most celebrated heritage corridor, home to the
world-renowned Top Tourist Places in Rajasthan. This journey is equally popular as an unforgettable Rajasthan
Honeymoon Package or an anniversary celebration, thanks to its stunning landscapes and royal backdrops. From
exploring massive hilltop forts and floating lake palaces to discovering popular Instagram locations, our customized
TRAGUIN Rajasthan Packages offer something special for everyone. Enjoy colorful local puppet shows, vibrant
historic bazaars, private sunset boat cruises, and handpicked heritage hotels that ensure your family vacation is
comfortable, safe, and truly magical. 
BESPOKE DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN JAIPUR
WELCOME TO THE PINK CITY – TRADITIONAL GREETINGS & MEMORABLE CULTURAL
NIGHT
Your grand family holiday begins with a warm welcome. Upon arriving at Jaipur Airport, your family will receive a
traditional royal greeting arranged by our dedicated TRAGUIN coordinators. Your professional chauffeur will assist
with your luggage and drive you to your premium handpicked hotel in a spacious, air-conditioned vehicle. 
After check-in and some time to rest, enjoy an evening drive past the beautifully lit Albert Hall Museum, a wonderful
spot for a family group photo. Continue to Chokhi Dhani, a famous ethnic village resort, to experience traditional
Rajasthani hospitality with folk dances, puppet shows, camel rides for the children, and an authentic, multi-course local
dinner.

DAY 02 | JAIPUR FULL-DAY SIGHTSEEING
FORTRESSES & RULERS – AMER FORT RESIDENCE & PALACE OF WINDS
Start your day with a delicious breakfast before heading out for a full day of Jaipur Sightseeing. Drive to the majestic
Amer Fort, located atop the rugged hills of the Aravali range. Walk through its grand courtyards and admire the
incredible mirrored ceilings of the Sheesh Mahal while listening to engaging stories from your expert guide. On the way
back, stop for a family photo in front of the iconic Jal Mahal, a beautiful palace sitting in the center of Man Sagar Lake. 
In the afternoon, explore the City Palace complex, which blends Rajput and Mughal architecture, and visit the adjacent
Jantar Mantar observatory to see ancient stone astronomical instruments that fascinate guests of all ages. Wrap up the
afternoon with a photo stop at the famous Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). 
Sightseeing Included: Amer Fort, Jal Mahal View, City
Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal.
Optional Activities: Private block-printing workshop for the
family.
Evening Experience: Shopping walk through Johari and
Bapu Bazaars.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur.
DAY 03 | JAIPUR TO JODHPUR
HEADING TO THE DESERT KINGDOM – THE MAJESTIC BLUE CITY ARRIVAL
Enjoy a relaxed morning breakfast before embarking on a smooth drive west toward Jodhpur, the historic "Blue City" of
Rajasthan. The journey features well-paved highways with pre-planned stops for refreshments at clean, family-friendly
boutique spots. Watch the changing landscape transition into the semi-arid beauty of Marwar. 
Arrive in Jodhpur by late afternoon and check into your premium heritage hotel. Spend the evening relaxing with your
family by the pool or walking through the property's beautiful lawns, taking in views of the majestic Mehrangarh Fort
illuminated against the night sky. 
Sightseeing Included: Countryside landscape drive, heritage
hotel exploration.
Optional Activities: Traditional high tea featuring classic
Marwari sweets.
Evening Experience: A relaxed sit-down dinner on a
panoramic rooftop terrace.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur (Premium Palace Hotel).

CITADELS OF POWER – MEHRANGARH FORT FORTSPACE & MARBLE MONUMENTS
Dedicate your day to exploring the best of Jodhpur Sightseeing. Visit the massive Mehrangarh Fort, which stands 400
feet above the city skyline. Walk through its grand palaces, including the Moti Mahal and Phool Mahal, and visit its
museums to see royal palanquins, armor, and artifacts. Next, visit Jaswant Thada, a peaceful white marble memorial set
amidst calm lakeside gardens, perfect for a family stroll. 
In the afternoon, enjoy a private jeep safari to a nearby Bishnoi village. Meet local artisans, observe traditional block
printers and potters, and spot local wildlife like blackbucks and migratory birds, offering an unforgettable experience for
children. 
Sightseeing Included: Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada,
Umaid Bhawan Palace Outer View.
Optional Activities: Bishnoi Village Jeep Safari.
Evening Experience: A walk past the historic Clock Tower
and Sadar Market.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur.
DAY 05 | JODHPUR TO UDAIPUR VIA RANAKPUR
SACRED ARCHITECTURE – MARBLE MARVELS EN ROUTE TO THE LAKESIDE
Bid farewell to Jodhpur after breakfast as you travel south toward Udaipur. En route, stop in a quiet valley of the Aravali
range to visit the magnificent 15th-century Ranakpur Jain Temple. This architectural masterpiece features 1,444 uniquely
carved marble pillars, where no two designs are exactly alike—a beautiful and peaceful highlight for the family. 
Continue your scenic drive to Udaipur, the romantic "Venice of the East." Check into your handpicked lakeside resort
and enjoy a free evening to relax after the journey. 
Sightseeing Included: Ranakpur Jain Temple, Aravali
Mountain Pass Drive.
Optional Activities: Lunch stop featuring authentic local
cuisine.
Evening Experience: Relaxing lakeside walk at your resort.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur (Premium Lakeside Resort).
DAY 06 | UDAIPUR FULL-DAY SIGHTSEEING
THE VENICE OF THE EAST – LAKESIDE CITY PALACE & UNFORGETTABLE CRUISE
Discover the best of Udaipur Sightseeing today. Visit the magnificent City Palace complex, the largest in Rajasthan,
sitting right on the banks of Lake Pichola. Walk through its grand rooms, mirrored galleries, and central courtyards.
Next, visit the historic Jagdish Temple and Saheliyon-ki-Bari (the Garden of Maidens), a beautiful park filled with
fountains, lotus pools, and marble elephants that children love. 
In the late afternoon, enjoy a private family boat cruise on Lake Pichola. Watch the sunset illuminate the city, the grand
City Palace, and the beautiful island palace of Jag Mandir—a scenic highlight that makes for a perfect final family photo.
