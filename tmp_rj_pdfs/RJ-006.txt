Your spectacular Jaipur Sightseeing journey begins with a traditional royal welcome at Jaipur Airport, meticulously

DAY 02 | JAIPUR FULL DAY SIGHTSEEING
MAJESTIC FORTS & THE GLITTERING CROWN JEWELS OF RAJASTHAN
After a delicious morning breakfast, head out to explore the grand Amber Fort, a beautiful hill fortress featuring
magnificent mirrors and marble courtyards. Stop on the way to take photographs at the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of
Winds). Later, explore the grand City Palace complex, which still houses the royal family, and marvel at the historic
astronomical instruments at Jantar Mantar, a UNESCO World Heritage site. 
Sightseeing Included: Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City
Palace, Jantar Mantar, Jal Mahal.
Optional Activities: Exclusive private shopping tour for
authentic jewelry and block prints.
Evening Experience: Gourmet dinner at an award-winning
fort restaurant.
Overnight Stay: Jaipur Palace Hotel.
DAY 03 | JAIPUR TO JODHPUR VIA AJMER/PUSHKAR
SCENIC HIGHWAYS & SACRED FAITH TRAILS INTO THE SUN CITY
Leave Jaipur after breakfast for a scenic drive toward Jodhpur. En route, stop at the holy town of Ajmer to visit the
historic Khwaja Gharib Nawaz Dargah, or visit Pushkar’s sacred lake and the rare, ancient Lord Brahma Temple.
Afterward, continue your drive through the changing landscapes into the grand Blue City of Jodhpur. Check into your
luxury palace hotel and spend the evening relaxing. 
Sightseeing Included: Ajmer Dargah / Pushkar Holy Lake
circuit.
Optional Activities: Sunset camel rides on Pushkar's gentle
desert dunes.
Evening Experience: Relaxation by the hotel's heritage
pool.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur (Premium Palace Stay).
DAY 04 | JODHPUR FULL DAY SIGHTSEEING
EXPLORING THE MIGHTY MEHRANGARH CITADEL & INTENSE BLUE LANES
Spend your day exploring the absolute highlights of Jodhpur Sightseeing. Visit the mighty Mehrangarh Fort, towering
400 feet over the city skyline, and discover its grand palaces and rich historical museums. Next, stroll through Jaswant
Thada, a peaceful white marble cenotaph set next to quiet lakes. In the afternoon, enjoy an immersive walking tour
through the old city’s indigo-hued streets, a popular Instagram location for travelers. 
Sightseeing Included: Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, Clock
Tower, Umaid Bhawan Museum.
Optional Activities: Adventurous zip-lining over the
majestic fort battlements.
Evening Experience: Romantic rooftop dinner overlooking the
illuminated fort walls.
Overnight Stay: Jodhpur Heritage Resort.

DAY 05 | JODHPUR TO UDAIPUR VIA RANAKPUR
EXQUISITE MARBLE SCULPTURES & ARRIVAL IN THE VENICE OF THE EAST
Travel from Jodhpur toward the romantic city of Udaipur. On the way, stop to explore the 15th-century Ranakpur Jain
Temple, hidden in a quiet valley within the Aravali hills. Admire its 1,444 uniquely carved marble pillars. Afterward,
continue your drive through beautiful valleys into Udaipur, the famous City of Lakes, and check into your breathtaking
luxury lakeside hotel. 
Sightseeing Included: Ranakpur Jain Temple, Aravali Valley Drive.
Optional Activities: Authentic Mewari lunch stop.
Evening Experience: A peaceful lakeside walk at sunset.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur (Premium Handpicked Hotel).
DAY 06 | UDAIPUR PALACE TOUR & LAKE PICHOLA CRUISE
THE GRAND MEWAR REGALITY & ROMANTIC SUNSET BOAT CRUISES
Immerse yourself in a full-day Udaipur Sightseeing experience. Explore the magnificent City Palace complex,
showcasing grand mirrors, mosaics, and lovely interior courtyards. Visit Jagdish Temple and walk through Saheliyon-ki-
Bari (the Garden of Maidens). In the late afternoon, enjoy a peaceful, private boat cruise on Lake Pichola, offering
stunning views of Jag Mandir Palace against the golden sunset. 
Sightseeing Included: City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon-ki-
Bari, Lake Pichola Cruise.
Optional Activities: Elegant evening folk dance show
at Bagore Ki Haveli.
Evening Experience: Fine dining at a spectacular lakeside
restaurant.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur Lakeside Palace.
DAY 07 | UDAIPUR SECONDARY LAKES & HILL FORT EXCURSION
MONSOON PALACE PANORAMAS & LAKESIDE CAFE IMMERSIONS
Drive up the mountains to visit the Monsoon Palace (Sajjangarh), which offers incredible views of Udaipur's lakes and
countryside. Spend your afternoon relaxing near the quiet shores of Fateh Sagar Lake, exploring its beautiful parks and
boutique lakeside cafés. This day is designed to give you a relaxing experience, allowing you to capture memories and
fully enjoy your luxury holiday. 
Sightseeing Included: Sajjangarh Palace, Fateh Sagar Lake, Nehru Park.
Optional Activities: Vintage Car Museum tour.
Evening Experience: Custom couples' or family photography session.
Overnight Stay: Udaipur Lakeside Palace.
