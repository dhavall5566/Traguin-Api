DESTINATION SEO INSIGHT & EXPLORATION
Sasan Gir is universally recognized as the jewel of wildlife tourism in Western India. It is highly sought after by
luxury travelers, international wildlife photographers, and multi-generational families looking for an elite
vacation. The region's rich bio-diversity includes more than 300 species of birds, the elusive Indian leopard,
striped hyenas, and mugger crocodiles. 
The absolute pinnacle of your Gujarat Sightseeing journey is the exclusive Gir Jungle Safari, widely cited as
one of the most searched experiences in worldwide tourism. Our premium TRAGUIN Gujarat Packages are
tailored to position you right at the heart of the wilderness during the Best Time to Visit Gujarat. Capture
incredible shots at popular Instagram locations inside the forest, indulge in organic farm-to-table culinary
experiences, and engage in deeply moving interactions with the local Maldhari tribespeople. This is not just a
holiday; it is an immersive premium experience.

IMPORTANT NOTES
Safari Permit Requirements: Gir safari permits are subject to availability and strictly regulated by
government authorities. We strongly suggest booking at least 60-90 days in advance.
Identity Verification: It is mandatory to carry the exact original government identity proof (Passport/
Aadhaar) submitted at the time of booking for park entry check-points.
Park Operational Cycle: Please note Sasan Gir National Park core zones remain closed annually from
16th June to 15th October due to monsoon seasons. Devalia Safari Park remains open throughout the
year.
Appropriate Dress Code: Wear neutral color tones (khaki, olive, brown, grey) during the safaris to
seamlessly blend with the natural surroundings. Avoid bright or neon shades.
