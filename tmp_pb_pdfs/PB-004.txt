DAY 02 | AMRITSAR CITY SIGHTSEEING TO WAGAH BORDER
HISTORIC SACRIFICES & HIGH-OCTANE MILITARY PATRIOTISM
After a delicious breakfast, pay a solemn visit to the historic Jallianwala Bagh to honor the martyrs of freedom.
Next, explore the grand Partition Museum, which stands as a deeply moving repository of historical memories.
In the afternoon, take a scenic drive to the Wagah Border to witness the legendary international Border
Beating Retreat Ceremony. Feel the intense patriotic energy, loud cheers, and precise military choreography
before returning to the city for local shopping.

DAY 03 | AMRITSAR TO KAPURTHALA HERITAGE EXPEDITION
THE PARIS OF PUNJAB – FRENCH ARCHITECTURE & ROYAL COURTS
Depart Amritsar after a hearty breakfast and travel to the majestic princely state of Kapurthala, fondly called
the 'Paris of Punjab'. Admire the spectacular Jagatjit Palace, inspired by the Palace of Versailles in France.
Explore the beautiful Elysee Palace, the unique Moorish Mosque built by a French architect, and the stunning
Shalimar Gardens. This city offers popular Instagram locations for capturing unique heritage portraits.

SHOPPING & LOCAL EXPERIENCES
Local Bazaars & Souvenirs: Visit Katra Jaimal Singh bazaar to find genuine hand-embroidered Phulkari
dupattas and suits. Amritsar is also world-famous for its delicious spiced papads, traditional warian, and fine
hand-tooled leather Punjabi Juttis. 
Cafes & Food: Beyond traditional roadside dhabas, explore Amritsar’s high-end local cafes to enjoy authentic
lassi variants, hot jalebis cooked in pure desi ghee, and rich slow-cooked black dal makhani.
