DETAILED DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AMRITSAR
WELCOME TO THE SPIRITUAL HEART – EVENING PALK SAHIB CEREMONY
Your highly anticipated Punjab getaway begins with a VIP arrival experience. Your professional, uniform-clad
chauffeur welcomes your family at the Amritsar Airport or Railway Station in a private luxury vehicle. Transfer
smoothly to your handpicked premium stay. After checking in and enjoying a short rest, step out into the
spiritual center of the city. As dusk falls, visit the shimmering Golden Temple to witness the majestic,
emotionally moving Palki Sahib Ceremony, where the holy Guru Granth Sahib is carried in a golden
palanquin. The stunning gold reflections on the sacred pool offer a perfect photography point.

DAY 02 | HISTORIC AMRITSAR & WAGAH BORDER CEREMONY
PATRIOTIC FERVOR, EMOTIONAL STORYTELLING & ROYAL BORDER BEATING
RETREAT
Relish a lavish breakfast before diving into deep historical storytelling at Jallianwala Bagh, a memorial park
holding the profound spirit of India's freedom struggle. Next, view the innovative Partition Museum to witness a

dinner experience.

DAY 03 | CULTURAL IMMERSION AT SADDA PIND
TRADITIONAL VILLAGE LIFE, LIVELY FOLK DANCES & PUNJABI HOSPITALITY
Enjoy your morning breakfast at the hotel, followed by a visit to the Maharaja Ranjit Singh Panorama and
Summer Palace museum. By afternoon, journey to Sadda Pind, an expansive living Punjabi heritage village
museum. This immersive experience lets your family interact with traditional artisans, witness energetic
Bhangra and Gidda folk performances, watch local magic shows, and try fun activities like pottery making. It is
a highly popular Instagram location filled with color and joy. 
Sightseeing Included:
Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum, Sadda Pind Cultural Village entry.
Evening Experience:
A grand traditional village fair experience with folk music and dance circles.
Overnight Stay:
Amritsar (Premium / Luxury Selected Hotel)
Meals Included:

SHOPPING & LOCAL EXPERIENCES
Local Markets & Famous Shopping: Explore the heritage markets around Katra Jaimal Singh and Hall
Bazaar. These bustling streets are famous for authentic hand-crafted Phulkari dupattas, fine pashmina
shawls, traditional wedding ornaments, and embroidered Punjabi Juttis. 
Cafes & Food Recommendations: Indulge your family in iconic dining at Kesar Da Dhaba or Bharawan Da
Dhaba for authentic Punjabi thalis. Sip on thick, creamy lassi served in traditional tall clay glasses, and don't
miss the chance to try hot jalebis in the old city squares.
