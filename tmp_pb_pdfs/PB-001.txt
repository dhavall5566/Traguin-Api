IMPORTANT NOTES & TRAVEL INFORMATION
Temple Protocol: It is mandatory to keep your head covered at all times inside the temple complex. Scarf
cloths are available at the entrance. Footwear must be deposited at the complimentary counters outside.
Wagah Border Security: Bags, purses, or luggage items are strictly prohibited at the Wagah Border
stadium due to state security protocols. Only wallets and mobile phones are allowed.
Advance Booking: Booking your custom itinerary 30 days in advance is highly recommended to secure
prime room availability during weekends.
