DAY 04 | JALANDHAR TO PATIALA
THE ROYAL COUTURE – FORTIFIED PALACES & RECOGNIZED ROYALTY
Travel south toward the royal city of Patiala, world-famous for its majestic turbans, parandas, and traditional
palaces. Explore the glorious Qila Mubarak Complex, an incredible example of Sikh palace architecture. Tour
the Sheesh Mahal (Palace of Mirrors) and marvel at the breathtaking reflections and historical royal artifacts.
This city is a highly popular Instagram location for capturing royal portraits. 
Sightseeing Included:
Qila Mubarak, Sheesh Mahal, Baradari Gardens, Gurudwara Dukh Niwaran Sahib.
Evening Experience:
A guided walk through local craft bazaars to buy authentic Patiala Shahi Juttis.
Overnight Stay:
Patiala (Premium Heritage Hotel Stay)
Meals Included:
Breakfast & Royal Heritage Dinner
