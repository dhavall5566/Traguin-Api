DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN AMRITSAR
WELCOME TO THE HOLY CITY – THE GLOW OF THE GOLDEN TEMPLE
Your premium Punjab experience begins as you arrive at Amritsar International Airport, where a private luxury
transport SUV waits to escort you. Check into your ultra-luxury handpicked hotel and refresh. In the late
afternoon, experience an emotional storytelling tour of Jallianwala Bagh. As dusk falls, proceed to the glorious
Sri Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple) to witness the breath-taking landscapes of the illuminated pool of
nectar and the spiritual seat of Sri Akal Takht Sahib (Takht 1).

Sri Akal Takht Sahib, Sri Harmandir Sahib, Heritage Street Walk, Jallianwala Bagh.

Amritsar for a late-night street food tour to relish world-famous kulchas and rich lassi.

THE BIRTHPLACE OF KHALSA – SPIRIT OF COUARGE
Depart Amritsar after an early breakfast and travel via a beautiful highway route to the holy city of Anandpur
Sahib, framed by the majestic Shivalik hills. Explore Takht Sri Keshgarh Sahib (Takht 2), the sacred site where
the Khalsa Panth was founded in 1699 by Guru Gobind Singh Ji. Immerse yourselves in the history of the
ancient weapons preserved inside the shrine.
