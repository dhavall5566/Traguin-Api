WHY VISIT DESTINATION: THE PREMIUM ODISHA EXPERIENCE
When exploring choices for the Best Odisha Tour Package, discerning families choose an immersive dive
into architectural wonders and peaceful ecological sanctuaries. Odisha is widely famous for housing some of
the most legendary, iconic attractions in Asian history. From the colossal Jagannath Temple in Puri to the
architectural perfection of the Konark Sun Temple—a UNESCO World Heritage Site—the cultural depth here
is truly boundless. 
For couples planning a Odisha Honeymoon Package or families on a comprehensive Odisha Family Tour,
the state presents an amazing collection of highly popular Instagram locations. Capture the sunrise over the
Bay of Bengal, view the playful Irrawaddy Dolphins at Chilika Lake (the largest coastal lagoon in India), and
walk through the historic sandstone caves of Khandagiri and Udayagiri. With world-renowned applique
shopping at Pipli, ancient silver filigree art, and legendary culinary delights like the holy Mahaprasad, booking
one of our custom TRAGUIN Odisha Packages ensures you visit during the best time to visit Odisha with
absolute peace of mind. 
DETAILED DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN BHUBANESWAR
WELCOME TO THE TEMPLE CITY – ANCIENT STONE STORIES
Your premium Odisha sightseeing trip begins with a warm, personalized greeting at Bhubaneswar Airport or
Railway Station by your dedicated private chauffeur. Transfer comfortably to your premium handpicked hotel.
After refreshing, step out to discover the historic architectural legacy of the city. Visit the colossal 11th-century
Lingaraj Temple, the exquisite Rajarani Temple (famed for its erotic stone carvings), and Mukteswar Temple,
beautifully known as the "Gem of Odishan Architecture."

DAY 02 | BHUBANESWAR TO PURI VIA DHAULI & PIPLI
FROM PEACE JAIN CAVES TO THE SACRED COASTLINE
Enjoy a beautiful buffet breakfast before checking out. Visit the historic twin caves of Udayagiri and
Khandagiri, carved out as residential blocks for Jain monks in the 2nd century BCE. Next, drive to Dhauli Hills,
the emotional site of the ancient Kalinga War, where Emperor Ashoka renounced violence and embraced
Buddhism. Stop at the colorful heritage artisan village of Pipli to view live applique handicraft shopping before
arriving in the holy city of Puri to check into your luxury beachfront property. 
Sightseeing Included:
Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves, Dhauli Peace Pagoda, Pipli Artisan Street.

DAY 03 | PURI JAGANNATH TEMPLE & KONARK EXCURSION
SPIRITUAL BLESSINGS & THE CHARIOT OF THE SUN GOD
Awake early for an exclusive, deeply spiritual VIP darshan experience at the legendary Jagannath Temple,
built in the 12th century. Witness the immense devotion that defines this sacred icon. Return to the hotel for
breakfast, then drive along the breathtaking Marine Drive road to Konark. Stand in awe before the world-
famous Konark Sun Temple, a stunning structural marvel shaped like a gigantic stone chariot with 24
beautifully carved wheels. Return to Puri for a relaxed evening.

DAY 04 | EXCURSION TO CHILIKA LAKE (SATAPADA)
WETLAND MAJESTY & IMMERSIVE DOLPHIN CRUISING
Embark on a wonderful morning drive to Satapada on Chilika Lake, a top tourist place in Odisha for pristine
wildlife photography. Board an exclusive private boat cruise across the calm waters of the lake. Watch the
unique Irrawaddy Dolphins surfacing in their natural home and visit the unique Sea Mouth island where
Chilika Lake meets the vast Bay of Bengal. On your return trip, visit Raghurajpur Heritage Crafts Village to
witness the intricate ancient art of Pattachitra painting. 
Sightseeing Included:
Chilika Lake Private Boat Cruise, Dolphin Viewpoints, Raghurajpur Craft Village.
Evening Experience:
A beautiful cultural evening viewing live Gotipua or Odissi dance forms (on request).

Temple Dress Code: Jagannath Temple strictly enforces a traditional dress code (formal attire, no shorts
or sleeveless clothing allowed). Non-Hindus are restricted to outer precinct viewings.
Advance Booking: Booking 30–45 days in advance is highly recommended to secure premium ocean-
facing resort categories during peak season.
