WHY VISIT SATKOSIA GORGE? - THE ULTIMATE ODISHA ADVENTURE
When seeking the absolute Best Odisha Tour Package, adventure-focused travelers choose the dramatic
wilderness of the Satkosia Gorge Sanctuary. Famed for its unique biogeographic confluence, it stands out as an
iconic attraction for wildlife enthusiasts and luxury seekers alike. It is widely considered one of the top tourist places
in Odisha for spotting endangered gharials, freshwater crocodiles, elephants, and rare avian species, making it a
highly sought-after Odisha Family Tour or a unique Odisha Honeymoon Package destination. 
Satkosia offers breathtaking landscapes that provide popular Instagram locations, especially during the golden
hours along the sandbars of Badmul. Visitors can immerse themselves in thrilling experiences, such as deep-gorge
boat safaris, jungle canopy trekking, and cultural interactions with native forest-dwelling communities. Whether
you're interested in wildlife photography, star-gazing away from urban lights, or simply relaxing by a riverside
campfire, choosing the best time to visit Odisha ensures an elite getaway. Booking your luxury getaway through
specialized TRAGUIN Odisha Packages ensures premium handpicked hotels, experienced naturalists, and
completely tailored private itineraries.

DAY 02 | DEEP GORGE RIVER SAFARI & WILDLIFE OBSERVATION
CRUISING UNTAMED WATERS – GHARIALS & BREATHTAKING LANDSCAPES
Awake to the calling of wild birds echoing across the gorge. Today features an unforgettable, immersive experience.
Embark on a private morning boat safari into the deepest stretch of the Satkosia Gorge, where the Mahanadi River
cuts through 22 kilometers of sheer hill country. Keep your camera ready at key photography points to capture giant
freshwater mugger crocodiles and endangered gharials sunning themselves on the banks. In the afternoon, explore
deep forest trails guided by native trackers to spot herds of spotted deer, wild boars, and magnificent hornbills. 
Sightseeing Included:
Deep Gorge Private Boat Safari, Crocodile Conservation Center, Interpretation Zone.
Optional Activities:
Kayaking along the safer shallow river sandbars under professional supervision.
Overnight Stay:
Satkosia / Badmul (Premium Eco-Luxury Resort)
Meals Included:
Breakfast, Lunch & Theme Jungle Dinner
DAY 03 | JUNGLE CANOPY TREK & TRIBAL CULTURE ENCOUNTER
PRIMITIVE FORESTS & RITUALS OF THE LAND
Indulge in a premium breakfast before heading into the core buffer zone for an exhilarating jungle canopy trek.
Wander beneath towering sal and bamboo trees while your naturalist points out medicinal plants and tracks
pugmarks of large cats. In the afternoon, enjoy an exclusive experience visiting a nearby ethnic community village.
Witness their ancient sustainable lifestyle, admire hand-woven organic crafts, and learn about their deep cultural
relationship with the surrounding wildlife forest. 
Sightseeing Included:
Canopy Walkways, Eco-Village Tour, Wildlife watchtower lookouts.
Evening Experience:
Private sandbar bonfire featuring tribal folk dance performances and skewered local delicacies.
Overnight Stay:
Satkosia / Badmul (Premium Eco-Luxury Resort)
Meals Included:
Breakfast, Gourmet Lunch & Open-air Barbecue Dinner
DAY 04 | SATKOSIA TO BHUBANESWAR via REBANA REBAPI HERITAGE VIEW
RETURNING TO THE TEMPLE CITY – HISTORIC SPLENDOUR
Bid farewell to the wilderness after breakfast and drive back towards Bhubaneswar, the storied "Temple City of
India." Break your journey at an iconic attraction along the route to witness traditional stone carving workshops.
Upon arrival in Bhubaneswar, check into your ultra-luxury heritage city hotel. Spend your late afternoon visiting the
ancient Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves, exploring ancient rock-cut architecture that serves as a popular Instagram
location.
