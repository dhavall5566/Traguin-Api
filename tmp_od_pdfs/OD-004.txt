DETAILED DAY-WISE ITINERARY
DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN BHUBANESWAR – THE TEMPLE CITY
ANCIENT ARCHITECTURE & SACRED STONE CARVINGS
Your premium Odisha experience commences as you land at Biju Patnaik International Airport or arrive at
Bhubaneswar Railway Station. A dedicated private luxury transport vehicle waits to escort you safely. Check
into your premium hotel and prepare to discover the ancient temple capital. Visit the majestic 11th-century
Lingaraj Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, which showcases unmatched architectural grandeur. Proceed to the
beautifully carved Mukteswar Temple (known as the gem of Odishan architecture) and the unique circular
Rajarani Temple set amidst beautifully manicured lawns.

DAY 02 | BHUBANESWAR TO PURI VIA DHAULI & RAGHURAJPUR
THE PATH OF PEACE & IMMERSIVE HERITAGE CRAFT VILLAGES
After a morning breakfast, travel towards the holy city of Puri. En route, stop at Dhauli Shanti Stupa, a
magnificent white peace pagoda atop the hills where Emperor Ashoka renounced warfare and embraced
Buddhism. Following this emotionally moving stop, continue to Raghurajpur Heritage Craft Village. Wander
through this living museum where every household practices traditional Pattachitra scroll painting. Interact
with master craftsmen and witness timeless folk traditions before driving to your premium beach resort in Puri.

DAY 03 | DEVOTION AT THE MAJESTIC JAGANNATH TEMPLE
DIVINE BLESSINGS & UNMATCHED SPIRITUAL VIBRATIONS
Savor a sacred morning as you head to the iconic Shree Jagannath Temple, the heart of the Puri Temple

DAY 04 | CHILIKA LAKE DOLPHIN SANCTUARY EXCURSION
BREATHTAKING LANDSCAPES & PRISTINE COASTAL WETLANDS
Awake early to enjoy a beautiful sunrise over the Bay of Bengal, then drive to Satapada on Chilika Lake—
Asia's largest brackish water lagoon. This area provides breathtaking landscapes and a sanctuary for rare
Irrawaddy Dolphins. Board a private, luxury-outfitted boat charter to cruise the expansive waters, spot playful
dolphins, and visit Sea Mouth where the lake merges with the open ocean. Return to Puri by afternoon to
catch a beautiful sunset. 
Sightseeing Included:

DAY 05 | PURI TO KONARK SUN TEMPLE – BHUBANESWAR DEPARTURE
THE STONE CHARIOT OF THE SUN GOD – HOMEWARD JOURNEY
Following a lavish breakfast, check out and drive along the scenic Marine Drive towards Konark. Tour the
world-famous Konark Sun Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site sculpted as a massive chariot for the Sun
God, featuring 24 intricately carved stone wheels. Absorb the emotional storytelling behind this masterpiece
before continuing to Chandrabhaga Beach. Later, your private vehicle will transport you back to Bhubaneswar
Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey, leaving you with unforgettable memories designed by
TRAGUIN.

SHOPPING & LOCAL EXPERIENCES
Traditional Souvenirs: Take home authentic, brilliant Sambalpuri and Ikkat silk sarees from government-
approved emporiums. Don't miss out on miniature stone statues, Pipli applique wall hangings, and authentic
Pattachitra paintings directly from artists. 
Culinary Recommendations: Taste the legendary hot, syrup-filled Khaja sweets near the Jagannath Temple,
explore authentic Odia thalis, or enjoy fresh seafood delicacies at high-end coastal diners. 
IMPORTANT NOTES & TRAVEL INFORMATION
Temple Policies: Non-Hindus are strictly not permitted inside the main sanctum of the Puri Jagannath
Temple as per ancient local regulations. Leather items and mobile phones are prohibited inside temple
premises.
Dress Code: Traditional, conservative attire (sarees/salwars for women, dhotis/kurta-pyjamas or formal
trousers for men) is mandatory for temple darsan.
Advance Booking: We strongly recommend booking 45 days in advance during winter months to secure
ocean-facing suites.
