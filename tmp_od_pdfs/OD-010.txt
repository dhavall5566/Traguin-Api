DAY 02 | BHUBANESWAR LOCAL SIGHTSEEING
ANCIENT CAVES, JAIN HERITAGE & ROYAL WHITE PAGODAS
Relish a lavish morning buffet breakfast before departing for the twin hills of Khandagiri and Udayagiri.
Explore the ancient rock-cut caves adorned with dynamic historical inscriptions dating back to the 2nd century
BC. Following a premium lunch break, drive to Dhauli Hills—the emotional site of the Kalinga War where
Emperor Ashoka embraced Buddhism. Behold the majestic Shanti Stupa (White Peace Pagoda) overlooking
the serene Daya River, a highly popular Instagram location.

DAY 04 | PURI TO KONARK SUN TEMPLE EXCURSION
THE STONE POETRY OF THE SUN CHARIOT & RAMCHANDI BEACH
Drive along the stunning, scenic marine drive road towards Konark to witness the awe-inspiring Konark Sun
Temple. Conceived as a colossal stone chariot with 24 beautifully carved wheels, this 13th-century wonder
showcases breathtaking landscapes of architectural precision. Hear emotional storytelling of ancient
craftsmen from our expert guide. Later, unwind at the tranquil Ramchandi Beach and the confluence of the
Kushabhadra River. 
Sightseeing Included:
Konark Sun Temple, Konark Interpretation Centre, Ramchandi Beach, Chandrabhaga
Beach.

DAY 05 | EXCURSION TO CHILIKA LAKE (SATAPADA)
THE EMBRACE OF ASIA'S LARGEST BRACKISH LAGOON
Embark on a spectacular morning excursion to Satapada, the eastern section of the magnificent Chilika Lake.
Board an exclusive private boat ride arranged for your family to explore the expansive waters. Spot the rare,
playful Irrawaddy Dolphins swimming in their natural habitat. Cruise towards the Sea Mouth where the vast
lagoon meets the roaring Bay of Bengal—a truly panoramic, immersive experience. 
Sightseeing Included:
Chilika Lake Lagoon Cruise, Irrawaddy Dolphin Sanctuary points, Sea Mouth island
stopover.

THE LEGACY OF PATACHITRA MASTER ARTISANS
