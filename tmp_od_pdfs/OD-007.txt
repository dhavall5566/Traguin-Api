SHOPPING & LOCAL EXPERIENCES
Local Markets & Handicrafts: Shop for stunning, authentic Sambalpuri and Ikkat silk sarees directly from
certified weavers. Odisha is also globally famous for beautiful silver filigree work (Tarakasi) from Cuttack and
intricate Pattachitra paintings. 
Cafes & Food: Indulge in traditional coastal sweets like soft, warm Pahala Rasgullas, legendary Chhena
Poda (baked cottage cheese dessert), and mild coconut-based curries at premium dining venues. 
IMPORTANT NOTES & TRAVEL INFORMATION
Temple Rules: Leather items, cameras, and mobile phones are strictly prohibited inside the Jagannath
Temple premises. Dress code is traditional/conservative.
Weather Notes: The winter season offers a beautiful coastal breeze. We recommend carrying a light
shawl or sweater for late evenings.
Advance Booking: Due to heavy demand for beachside luxury properties, we recommend booking 45
days in advance.
