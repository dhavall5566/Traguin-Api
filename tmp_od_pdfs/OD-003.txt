WHY CHOOSE THE BEST ODISHA TOUR PACKAGE?
When considering a Luxury Odisha Holiday, discerning families and devotees seek a smooth, well-guided
spiritual experience completely free from transit hassles. Odisha is home to some of the most sacred and
iconic attractions in the world. The holy city of Puri serves as one of the revered Char Dham pilgrimage sites,
making an Odisha Pilgrimage Tour or a dedicated Puri Jagannath Package a deeply significant life event
for families. From the awe-inspiring Sun Temple of Konark—a UNESCO World Heritage site—to the historic
temple city of Bhubaneswar, Odisha sightseeing offers profound peace and cultural richness. 
For travelers planning a meaningful family getaway, the region boasts beautiful Instagram locations like the
golden sands of Puri Beach, the exquisite carvings of Mukteshwar Temple, and the mesmerizing migratory
birds of Chilika Lake. Whether you are seeking local handloom shopping for beautiful Sambalpuri silk sarees,
tasting the legendary Mahaprasad, or experiencing the cultural richness of heritage craft villages, our
specialized TRAGUIN Odisha Packages guarantee handpicked hotels, premium stays, and curated exclusive
experiences during the best time to visit Odisha.

DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN BHUBANESWAR TO PURI
WELCOME TO THE TEMPLE CITY & PROCEED TO THE SACRED COAST
Your premium Odisha experience begins as you land at Biju Patnaik International Airport or arrive at
Bhubaneswar Railway Station. A designated private luxury transport vehicle will greet you warmly and escort

DAY 02 | PURI JAGANNATH DARSHAN & KONARK EXCURSION
DIVINE DARSHAN & THE MAGNIFICENT STONE CHARIOT OF THE SUN GOD
Awake early for the ultimate high point of your pilgrimage—the sacred Darshan at the Shree Jagannath
Temple. Guided by a registered empanelled temple priest, experience the grand spiritual aura of the deities.
Witness the iconic Neela Chakra and the bustling temple kitchens. Return to the resort for a rich breakfast,

13th-century stone chariot showcasing spectacular structural engineering. Conclude your day at
Chandrabhaga Beach, a serene and popular photography point.

DAY 03 | EXCURSION TO CHILIKA LAKE (SATAPADA)
BREATHTAKING LANDSCAPES, DOLPHINS & HERITAGE ART VILLAGES
Embark on a refreshing morning drive to Satapada on Chilika Lake, Asia's largest brackish water lagoon.
Board a private premium motorized boat tailored exclusively for your family to explore the expansive,
breathtaking landscapes. Keep your cameras ready at the premier photography points to spot the rare and
playful Irrawaddy Dolphins leaping in their natural habitat. On the return drive, stop at Raghurajpur Heritage
Craft Village, where every home is an art studio preserving ancient Pattachitra paintings.

paintings straight from rural artisans. 
Food Recommendations: Indulge in authentic temple sweets like Khaja, fresh coastal sea-food varieties at
premium resorts, and traditional Odia delicacies like Dalma and Chennapoda. 
IMPORTANT NOTES & TRAVEL INFORMATION
Temple Policies: Non-Hindus are restricted from entering the inner sanctum of Lingaraj and Jagannath
Temples. Leather items and mobile phones are strictly banned inside. Dress codes require traditional,
conservative attire.
Weather Notes: Travel during October to March offers exceptionally pleasant coastal weather. Light cotton
wear is highly recommended.
Advance Booking: We strongly advise booking 30–45 days ahead to lock in top premium beachfront
resort blocks.
