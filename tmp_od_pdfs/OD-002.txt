DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN BHUBANESWAR TO PURI
THE TEMPLE CITY TO THE DIVINE COASTLINE – WELCOME EXPERIENCE
Your premium Odisha experience begins with a grand reception at Bhubaneswar Airport or Railway Station by
your private luxury transport chauffeur. Embark on a highly scenic road route to Puri. En-route, visit the
mesmerizing Dhauli Shanti Stupa, the famous edict rock site of Emperor Ashoka. Witness the incredible
artisan heritage village of Raghurajpur, globally renowned for Pattachitra scroll paintings. Arrive in Puri and
check into your premium handpicked hotel overlooking the ocean.

DAY 02 | SACRED PURI & CHILIKA LAKE EXCURSION
DIVINE BLESSINGS & THE BREATHTAKING DOLPHIN LAGOON
Awake to the soothing symphony of ocean waves. Early this morning, experience a spiritually elevating private

Board an exclusive premium private boat cruise to spot the rare Irrawaddy Dolphins and visit the tranquil Sea
Mouth where the lake merges into the Bay of Bengal.

DAY 03 | THE KONARK SUN TEMPLE EXCURSION
THE BLACK PAGODA – A MONUMENT OF RITUALISTIC CHRONOMETRY
Enjoy a spectacular breakfast before taking a drive along the legendary Marine Drive toward the UNESCO
World Heritage Site—the Konark Sun Temple. Built in the 13th century as a colossal chariot for the Sun God,
its architectural brilliance features intricately carved stone wheels that serve as precise sundials. After
exploring this breathtaking monument, spend your afternoon relaxing at the clean, quiet sands of
Chandrabhaga Beach, a highly popular Instagram location. 
Sightseeing Included:
Konark Sun Temple, Konark Interpretation Centre, Chandrabhaga Beach, Ramachandi
Temple.
Evening Experience:
VIP tickets to the spellbinding Konark Sound & Light multimedia show.
Overnight Stay:
Puri (Handpicked Premium Beachfront Resort)

DAY 04 | PURI TO BHUBANESWAR & DEPARTURE
HERITAGE CAVES TO RETURN JOURNEY – UNFORGETTABLE MEMORIES
Indulge in a final morning breakfast by the sea. Check out and drive back to Bhubaneswar to explore the
ancient rock-cut architecture of Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves, dating back to the 2nd century BCE. Visit the
beautiful Lingaraj Temple plaza before your private luxury vehicle smoothly escorts you to the airport or station
for your homeward journey. Bid farewell to the coast while carrying unforgettable memories meticulously
designed by TRAGUIN.
