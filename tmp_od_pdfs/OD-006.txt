tranquil coastal beauty, and untouched nature. Odisha presents some of the most iconic attractions in the
world. From the world-famous Jagannath Temple in Puri to the architectural brilliance of the Konark Sun

For families and couples booking an exclusive Odisha Honeymoon Package or Odisha Family Tour, the

DAY 01 | ARRIVAL IN BHUBANESWAR
WELCOME TO THE TEMPLE CITY – ANCIENT ARCHITECTURE & HERITAGE
Your premium Odisha experience begins as you touch down at Biju Patnaik International Airport, where a
dedicated private luxury transport vehicle waits to escort you. Check into your premium handpicked luxury
hotel and freshen up. In the afternoon, explore the magnificent 11th-century Lingaraj Temple and the unique
Mukteswar Temple, affectionately called the "Gem of Odishan Architecture." Capture stunning family portraits
among the beautifully preserved red sandstone lawns and carved arches.

DAY 02 | BHUBANESWAR TO PURI VIA DHAULI & PIPLI
THE PATH OF PEACE & THE CRAFT VILLAGE OF APPLIQUÉ ART
Indulge in a rich breakfast before embarking on a highly scenic drive to the holy coastal town of Puri. En route,
make an emotional stop at the Dhauli Peace Pagoda, the historic hill site where Emperor Ashoka renounced
warfare and embraced Buddhism. Next, walk through the vibrant, colorful streets of Pipli, a world-famous craft
village renowned for its exquisite handmade appliqué works—the perfect place for souvenir shopping. Arrive
in Puri and check into your luxury beach resort. Enjoy a peaceful sunset walk along the golden sand shores.

DAY 03 | HOLY PURI SIGHTSEEING & HERITAGE ENCOUNTERS
SACRED CHANTS, RITUALS & THE LIVING HERITAGE VILLAGE
Experience pure spiritual bliss with an early morning VIP-assisted visit to the sacred Lord Jagannath Temple,

DAY 04 | EXCURSION TO KONARK SUN TEMPLE
POETRY IN STONE & THE DRAMATIC MARINE DRIVE CAUSEWAY
Today, drive along the pristine, tree-lined Puri-Konark Marine Drive, one of India's most scenic coastal routes.
Arrive at the legendary Konark Sun Temple, a spectacular 13th-century chariot-shaped architectural wonder.
Marvel at the 24 massive, intricately carved stone wheels acting as sundials, and let your guide reveal the
hidden meaning behind the erotic and royal friezes. Conclude your evening at Chandrabhaga Beach, watching
the golden sun slide into the Bay of Bengal. 
Sightseeing Included:
Konark Sun Temple complex, Konark Interpretation Centre, Chandrabhaga Beach.
Evening Experience:
Mesmerizing multimedia light & sound history show projecting onto the temple structures.
Overnight Stay:
Puri (Premium Luxury Beach Resort)
Meals Included:
Premium Breakfast & Gourmet Dinner
DAY 05 | CHILIKA LAKE DOLPHIN CRUISE (SATAPADA)
BREATHTAKING LANDSCAPES & THE IRRAWADDY DOLPHINS
Embark on a morning drive to Satapada on Chilika Lake, a nature lover's paradise boasting breathtaking
landscapes. Board an exclusive private motorized boat to cruise across the glassy waters of this massive
lagoon. Spot the rare, playful Irrawaddy Dolphins leaping near the mouth of the sea. Visit the serene Sea
Mouth island where the lake merges into the ocean, providing incredible photography points for your family
album. 
Sightseeing Included:
Chilika Lagoon Boat Cruise, Irrawaddy Dolphin Spotting Zone, Sea Mouth Island.

ANCIENT CAVES & CHERISHING UNFORGETTABLE MEMORIES

traditional clay pots.
