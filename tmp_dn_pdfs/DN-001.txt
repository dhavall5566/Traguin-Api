WHY VISIT SILVASSA? THE ULTIMATE PREMIUM DADRA & NAGAR
HAVELI EXPERIENCE
When considering the Best Dadra & Nagar Haveli Tour Package, families and couples look for a
harmonious combination of undisturbed wildlife, colonial nostalgia, and modern waterfront relaxation.
Silvassa, the capital of this union territory, delivers exactly that. It stands out as a prime destination for an
elegant Dadra & Nagar Haveli Family Tour or an intimate, quiet Dadra & Nagar Haveli Honeymoon
Package. 
The region boasts magnificent attractions, such as the Vasona Lion Safari and the beautiful, cascading
terraces of Vanganga Lake Garden—consistently rated among the top tourist places in Dadra & Nagar Haveli.
Travelers seeking popular Instagram locations will be enchanted by the serene, misty mornings at Dudhani
Lake and the classic Portuguese design of Our Lady of Piety Church. Whether you are seeking water sports
adventure, shopping for authentic Warli paintings, or savoring local tribal cuisine, our TRAGUIN Dadra &
Nagar Haveli Packages represent the peak of eco-luxury travel during the best time to visit Dadra & Nagar
Haveli.

DAY 02 | SILVASSA TO VASONA SAFARI & KHANVEL
WILDLIFE MAJESTY & THE EMERALD FOREST HIDEAWAY
Indulge in a lavish breakfast before entering the thrilling wilderness of the Vasona Lion Safari. Board an iconic,
specially secured park vehicle for a close-range view of the majestic Asiatic lions roaming in their natural,
deep forest habitat. Next, drive deeper into the territory towards Khanvel, a tranquil valley flanked by
breathtaking landscapes and rich biodiversity. Spend your afternoon visiting the Deer Park at Satmaliya,
observing beautiful blackbucks and chitals roaming freely near the waterholes.

DAY 03 | EXCURSION TO DUDHANI LAKE
THE KASHMIR OF THE WEST – WATERFRONT LUXURY & WATER SPORTS
Today promises absolute scenic beauty as we venture to Dudhani Lake, a massive, stunning waterfront
formed by the impounded waters of the Madhuban Dam. Fondly called the 'Kashmir of the West', this lake is
surrounded by hills and dense forests. TRAGUIN has arranged an exclusive private speed-boat cruise for your
family to enjoy the refreshing lake breeze. Relax along the waterfront, capture beautiful sunset landscape
frames, and engage in thrilling water sports activities.
