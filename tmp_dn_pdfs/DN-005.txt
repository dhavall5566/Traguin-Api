DAY 04 | SILVASSA TO KHANVEL & LUHARI
SERENE JUNGLE CANOPIES & HISTORIC PORTUGUESE MEMORIES
Drive down the tree-lined avenues toward Khanvel, a beautiful pastoral landscape surrounded by lush green
forests. Visit the beautiful Bindrabin Temple, an ancient holy shrine situated elegantly on the banks of the

built by the Portuguese in 1889, showcasing spectacular stone architecture.
