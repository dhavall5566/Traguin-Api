WILD LIONS, THE MINI-KASHMIR WATERFRONT & SUNSET CRUISE
Treat yourself to a lavish breakfast before setting out for the thrilling Vasona Lion Safari. Board a secured

casts beautiful golden hues over the horizon.

craftspeople. Silvassa is also well known for premium hand-crafted bamboo mats, woven baskets, and
traditional herbal medical remedies sourced directly from the surrounding forests.

meals, and dynamic Portuguese-inspired treats available at premium resort bistros. 
IMPORTANT NOTES & TRAVEL INFORMATION
Advance Bookings: The Vasona Lion Safari remains closed on Mondays; the itinerary order may adjust
dynamically to optimize your viewing.
Weather Advice: Silvassa looks stunningly green post-monsoon and remains highly pleasant during
winters. Carrying casual linen clothes and comfortable walking shoes is recommended.
Hotel Rules: Original Government photo identification (Aadhar Card, Passport, or Driving License) must
be presented by all guests at check-in.
