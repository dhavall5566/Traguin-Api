SHOPPING & LOCAL EXPERIENCES
Local Markets & Souvenirs: Explore the old Lakkar Bazaar to purchase authentic wooden handicrafts,
custom-made walking sticks, exquisite Himachali shawls, and organic local fruit jams or honey. 
Cafes & Food: Old Shimla features classic wood-paneled cafes serving traditional English afternoon teas,
delicate fresh pastries, and authentic Himalayan-style comfort food. 
IMPORTANT NOTES & TRAVEL INFORMATION
Weather Notes: Evenings can be cold throughout the year; we recommend carrying light woolens for the
summer and heavy garments for winter.
Transport Rules: Motor vehicles are prohibited on Shimla's core Mall Road. The hotel provides dedicated
elevator assistance to ensure seamless transitions.
Advance Booking: We strongly recommend finalizing bookings 45–60 days in advance to secure prime
valley views at Oberoi properties.
