uncompromising modern luxury. Delhi is an incredible mosaic of empires, showcasing iconic attractions that
bridge centuries. From the towering majesty of the Qutub Minar and the architectural brilliance of Humayun's

SHOPPING & LOCAL EXPERIENCES
Local Markets & Souvenirs: Explore the heritage arcades of Connaught Place or Khan Market for premium
brands and books. Visit Dilli Haat for authentic Indian hand-woven textiles, sandalwood crafts, and intricate
traditional jewelry from various Indian states. 
Food & Cafes: Indulge in legendary Old Delhi street food like paranthas and chaat. For a modern vibe, visit
the chic cafes in Chanakyapuri or the upscale restaurants at the Lodhi colony for exquisite world cuisines.
